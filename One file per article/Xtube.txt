XTube waa websedyada adeeg hosting video Holland ku salaysan kaas oo u ogolaanaya dadka isticmaala si ay dad kale la wadaagaan video content dadka waaweyn. Waxyaabaha kale, shuruudaha adeegsiga gobolka xtube.com in dadka isticmaala waa in uu ahaadaa 18 sano jir ama ka weyn.

Tixraacyada
