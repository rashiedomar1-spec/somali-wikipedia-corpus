title:
Salidka Seepka

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=25483

text:
Goobta shidaalka ee batroolku waa meel dabiici ah ama qashinka dabiiciga ah uu aado jawiga dhulka iyo dusha sare, badiyaa cadaadis hoose ama socod. Carbarka carbuunku wuxuu ka baxsan karaa lakabyada jiilaalka, ama iyaga oo dhex maraya dhagxaanta ama dhagxaanta dhagaxa, ama si toos ah uga soo baxa dhagaxa saliida.
Beelaha batroolku waxay ku badan yihiin meelo badan oo adduunka ah, waxaana loo istcimaali jiray aadanaha tan iyo wakhtigii paleolithic. Waxyaabaha dabiiciga ah ee la xidhiidha suntan waxaa ka mid ah bitumen, pitch, &lt;a href="Asphaltka"&gt;Asphaltka&lt;/a&gt; iyo tar. Meelaha laga helo dabeecada dabiiciga ah ee dabiiciga ah, dabeecadda "dabka weligeed ah" ayaa inta badan sii jiraya. Xaaladda saliidda dusha sare ayaa inta badan lagu soo daray magacyada meelaha laga soo saaro; Goobahaasi waxay sidoo kale la xiriiraan saliidda iyo gaaska hore iyo sidoo kale horumarinta sayniska iyo farsamada, kuwaas oo koray warshadaha batroolka.
Hayira seeps.
