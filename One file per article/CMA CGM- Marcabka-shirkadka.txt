CMA CGM S.A. waa  shirkad faransiis oo qaada oona dhoofisa konteenarada. [ 3 ] Waa koox hoggaamisa maraakiibta adduunka oo dhan, [ 4 ] iyadoo adeegsaneeso 200 oo wadooyinka maraakiibta ah inta u dhaxaysa 420 dekedood oo ku kala yaal 150 waddan oo kala duwan [ 5 ] , waxay ku jirtaa darajada afaraad oo ka dambeysa Maersk Line, MSC iyo Khadadka Maraakiibta COSCO.
