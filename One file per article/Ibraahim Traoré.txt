Ibrahim Taroore, oo ah sarkaal ka tirsan milatariga &lt;a href="Burkina%20Faso"&gt;Burkina Faso&lt;/a&gt;, waxa uu dhashay &lt;a href="1988"&gt;1988&lt;/a&gt;, waxa uu ahaa hogaamiyihii ku meel gaadhka ahaa ee Burkina Faso tan iyo Sebtembar 30, &lt;a href="2022"&gt;2022&lt;/a&gt;, markii uu afgambiyay madaxwaynihii ku meel gaadhka ahaa ee &lt;a href="Paul-Henri%20Sandaogo%20Dameba"&gt;Paul-Henri Sandaogo Dameba&lt;/a&gt; intii lagu jiray &lt;a href="Afgambi%20burkiino%20faarso%202022"&gt;afgambigii Sebtembar 2022&lt;/a&gt; . Isla markiiba ka dib afgambigii, Taroore wuxuu ku dhawaaqay inuu yahay Madaxweynaha cusub &lt;a href="ee%20Dhaqdhaqaaqa%20Qaranka%20ee%20Ilaalinta%20iyo%20Dib-u-habaynta"&gt;ee Dhaqdhaqaaqa Qaranka ee Ilaalinta iyo Dib-u-habaynta&lt;/a&gt;, 06 Oktoobar 2022 sidoo kale wuxuu u qaatay jagada Madaxweyne ku meel gaadhka ah "Madaxa Qaranka, Madaxa Sare ee Ciidamada Qalabka Sida". Waxa uu ballan qaaday in uu qaban doono doorasho dimuqraadi ah Julaay 2024.
