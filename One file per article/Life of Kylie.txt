Life of Kylie waa taxane telefishan oo dhab ah oo Maraykan ah oo ay wax ka jilayso haweenay ganacsato ah Kylie Jenner iyo saaxiibkeed ugu wacnayd wakhtigaas, Jordyn Woods .
