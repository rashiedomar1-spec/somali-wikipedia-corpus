Taran Xisaab ahaan ( Af-Ingiriis : Product) waa natiijada Iskudhufasho laba walxoox. Kala hormarinta ay imaanayso tirooyin maangal ah ama kakan ee howlgalka iskudhufashada kumana lahan wax saamayn ah qiimaha taranta. Tani waa gaar ahaansho loola jeedo howsha iskudhufas bhada ee Kala-hormarinta.

Si kooban Taran waa wixi ka soo baxa laba tiro oo la isku dhufto sida 2*2= 4 markaa afar waa Taranta 2*2.
