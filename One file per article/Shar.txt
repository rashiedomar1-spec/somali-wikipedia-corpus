title:
Shar

url:

text:
Shar waxa ay tilmaamaysaa dabeecadaha ama falalka ama dhacdooyinka dabiiciga ah ee loo arko waxyeello, wax dumin ama anshax xumo oo ah isha dhibka akhlaaqda ama jidhka. Diimaha kala duwani waxay si kale u fasiraan waxa "shar". Falalka ama dabeecadaha qaar, sida &lt;a href="Dilka"&gt;dil&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="kufsi"&gt;kufsi&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="xatooyo"&gt;xatooyo&lt;/a&gt; ama &lt;a href="Jirdilka"&gt;jirdil&lt;/a&gt; ayaa loo arkaa in ay caalamku ka shar.
Ilaahay wuu arkaa shuqulladeenna xunxun.
