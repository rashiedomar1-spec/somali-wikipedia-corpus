Huang Xianfan (Fusui 13 Nofeembar 1899 - Guilin 18 Janaayo 1982 ), professor jaamacadda Shiinaha .Wuxuu dhashay taariikhdu markay aheyd 1899,wuxuuna ku dhashay Magaalada Fusui- ee Gobolka Guangxi, Shiinaha .Wuxuuna ifka uga tagay 9 caruur ah.

BUUGAAGTA UU QORAY: (PUBLICATIONS)

1913–1921:Dugsiga Hoose,dhexe: Qujiu.

1922–1926:Dugsiga macallin ee Nanning.

1926–1935:Jaamacadda macallin ee Beijing ( Beijing waa magaalo-madaxda dalka Shiinaha ).

1935–1937:Jaamacadda Tokyo (Tokyo waa magaalo-madaxda dalka Jabaan ) ee Jabaan .

1937–1938:wuxuu ahaa macallin dugsi sare ka dhiga juqraafi iyo taariikh ah.

1938–1940:wuxuu ahaa macallin Jaamacadda ee Guangxi ah.

1941–1942:wuxuu ahaa professor Jaamacadda ee SunJatsen ah.

1943–1953:wuxuu ahaa professor Jaamacadda ee Guangxi ah.

1954–1982:wuxuu ahaa professor Jaamacadda macallin ee Guangxi ah.

1932–1934:Wuxuu qoray: Taariikh Shiinaha , Ⅰ-Ⅲ, Beijing.

1957:Wuxuu qoray: Taariikh Zhuang,Nanning.
