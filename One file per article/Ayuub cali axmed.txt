Ayuub Cali axmed ( AF-ingiriisi : ayub ali ahmed ; dhashay 14 May 2000), waa aasaasaha blogger-ka somaliyeed ( Somali Blogger ) waxa uu sameeyay website badan sidoo kale waxa uu horumariyay Caawiye Q&A waa Somali professional web designer.

Noloshiisa

Ayuub Cali axmed wuxuu ku dhashay magaalada mogadishu taariikhda 14 May 2000 wuxuu wax ku bartay isla magaalada uuku dhashay wuxuu ka dhameeyay schoolka Ablaal secondary school waxa uu ka qalinjabiyay jaamacada somalia (uniso) sanadkii 2021.

Xirfadiisa

Ayuub waa xirfadle nashqadeeya websiteyada isaga oo udiyaariya si qurux badan waxaa uu nashqadeeyay in ka badaan 20-website
