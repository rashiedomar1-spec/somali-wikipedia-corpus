Melody / ɛ l ə d i / ; Janaayo 4, 2006) waa heesaa iyo heesaa reer Brazil ah, Melody waxa ay caan ku noqotay beentoodii.
Melody waxaa loo arkaa mid ka mid ah carruurta Brazil ee ugu saameynta badan intarneedka.
Sanadka 2022 Melody wuxuu noqonayaa fanaanka ugu da'da yar Latin America si uu u gaaro darajada Spotify ee ugu sareysa 200 ee heesta Pipoco. [ 1 ]
