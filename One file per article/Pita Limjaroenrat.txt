Pita Limjaroenrat (Thai: พิธา ลิ้มเจริญรัตน์; waxa uu dhashay 5 Sebtembar 1980) waa siyaasi iyo ganacsade Thai ah. Isagu waa hogaamiyaha xisbiga hore u socodka, dhab ahaantii ku guulaysta xisbiga mustaqbalka ee burburay.
