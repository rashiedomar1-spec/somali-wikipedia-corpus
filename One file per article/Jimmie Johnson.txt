Jimmie Kenneth Johnson (wuxuu ku dhashay &lt;a href="El%20Cajon%2C%20California"&gt;El Cajon, California&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="Sebtember%2017"&gt;Sebtember 17&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="1975"&gt;1975&lt;/a&gt;) waa xirfadle Mareykan ah oo ku tartamaya baabuurta.
Johnson wuxuu ciyaaray ciyaarihii NASCAR Cup ee sanadihii 2002 ilaa 2020 xilli uu la joogay kooxda &lt;a href="Hendrick%20Motorsports"&gt;Hendrick Motorsports&lt;/a&gt;. Taxanahan wuxuu ku guuleystay toddobo cinwaan oo kala ah 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2013 iyo 2016 wuxuuna barbareeyay rikoorka &lt;a href="Richard%20Petty"&gt;Richard Petty&lt;/a&gt; iyo &lt;a href="Dale%20Earnhardt"&gt;Dale Earnhardt&lt;/a&gt;.
Laga bilaabo 2021 Johnson wuxuu u guuray IndyCar si uu ugu biiro kooxda &lt;a href="Chip%20Ganassi%20Racing"&gt;Chip Ganassi Racing&lt;/a&gt;.
