Alexa Demie waa atariisho Mareykan ah iyo heesaa.  Waxay caan ku tahay doorkeeda Maddy Perez ee taxanaha HBO Euphoria .

Nolo

Demie waxay ku dhalatay Los Angeles, California ilaa Rose Mendez, farshaxan-qurxin ka timid Meksiko . [ 1 ] Waxay ku kortay Tuulada Atwater, waxaana inta badan hooyadeed ku soo korisay da'da siddeedaad, inkastoo ay sheegtay inay xiriir wanaagsan la leedahay aabaheed. [ 2 ]
