Koonfurta China Muranka Badda
koonfurta china muranka xadka halka xuduuda ay sheeganayaan dalal badan oo ka tirsan gobolka asia doorta umada qurbaha maraakiibta tamarta mara ee gobolka dhex mara dhamaan aduunka.
Waxaa dhacday dhacdooyin badan oo ka dhacay koonfurta china badda sheegashadeeda ku saabsan asias la doorto, mashiin taiwan oo ay toogteen hay'adda 'philappines navey' iyo dhacdooyin badan ayaa kadhacay sheegashada iyo lahaanshaha jasiiradaha.
