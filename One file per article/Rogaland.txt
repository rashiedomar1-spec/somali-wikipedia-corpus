Rogaland [²ruːɡɑlɑn] (ku saabsan codkan maqalka) waa gobolka gobolka Galbeedka &lt;a href="Norway"&gt;Norway&lt;/a&gt;, xuduudaha Hordaland, Telemark, Aust-Agder, iyo gobollada Vest-Agder. Rogaland ayaa ah xarunta dhexe ee batroolka Norway. Sannadkii 2016, Rogaland wuxuu lahaa shaqo la'aan 4.9%, mid ka mid ah kan ugu sareeya Noorway Sannadkii 2015, Rogaland wuxuu lahaa bacriminta 1.78 caruur ah oo haween ah, oo ah kan ugu sarreeya dalka.
Wareegga Stavanger ee Kaniisada Norway waxaa ka mid ah dhammaan gobolka Rogaland.
