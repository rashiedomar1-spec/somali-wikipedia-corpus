Boostada Masar Masar adeegga boostada ee masar ee masar oo gacanta ku haya waraaqaha gudaha iyo kuwa caalamiga ah, adeegga ugu weyn ee boostada ee masar ee africas electrat of Nations, ayaa la bilaabay 1800s sidii adeeg loogu diro waraaqaha adduunka oo dhan.
