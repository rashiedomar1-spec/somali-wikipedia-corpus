Tokyo Girls's Style (qaabaysan sida TOKYO GIRLS' STYLE ) waa koox gabdho Jabaan ah oo la aasaasay 2009, waxayna ahayd kooxdii ugu horreysay ee lagu abuuray Avex Trax kadib SweetS . Kooxdu waxay markii hore ka koobnayd shan xubnood: Miyu Yamabe, Hitomi Arai, Yuri Nakae, Konishi Ayano iyo Mei Shyoji. Bishii Diseembar 2015, Konishi Ayano wuxuu ku dhawaaqay inuu ka fariistay kooxda iyo warshadaha madadaalada.
