The Somaliland Qaranka Somaliland , rasmi ahaan Ciidanka SPSA ( Af-Inqiriisi Somaliland National Army , Af-Carabi   ), waa ciidanka dhulka iyo laanta ugu weyn ee ciidamada qalabka sida ee Somaliland waxay xaruntiisu tahay caasimada Somaliland ee Hargeysa . [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ]
