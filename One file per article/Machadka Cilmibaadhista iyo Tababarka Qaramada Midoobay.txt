Machadka Cilmibaadhista iyo Tababarka Qaramada Midoobay ( Af Ingiriis : United Nations Institute for Training and Research , loo soo gaabiyo: UNITAR ) ururkan waxaa la aasaasay sanadkii 1963 ka dib markii Sharciga Golaha Sare Qaramada Midoobay Archived Abriil 3, 2015 // Wayback Machine la ansixiyey, kaasi oo dhigayay in ururka QM iyo wakiiladiisa ineey helaan ilo warbixino iyo cilmibaadhis lagu kalsoon yahay oo ku saabsan xaqiiqada [ 2 ] ".
