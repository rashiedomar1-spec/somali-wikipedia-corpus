Jennifer Lynn " J.Lo " Lopez (Luuliyo 24, 1969) waa heesaa iyo atariisho Mareykan ah. 1991, Lopez waxa ay bilawday in ay ka soo muuqato taxanaha telefiishanka In Living Color , halkaas oo ay joogtay ilaa ay go'aansatay in ay sii wadato shaqadeeda jilida 1993. Doorkeedii ugu horeeyay hogaamineed ee filimkii 1997 Selena , Lopez waxa ay noqotay atariishii Laatiin ugu horaysay in la siiyo US$1 milyan. Waxay sii waday inay xiddig ka gasho Anaconda (1997) and Out of Sight (1998), waxayna isu taagtay inay noqoto aktarada Laatiinka ee ugu mushaarka badan Hollywood. [ 1 ]
