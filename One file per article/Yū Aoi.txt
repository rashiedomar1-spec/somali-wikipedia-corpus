Yu Aoi (蒼井 優, Aoi Yū ) waa atariisho reer Jabaan ah. Waxay sameysay kulankeedii ugu horreeyay ee film 2001-dii All About Lily Chou-Chou . Waxay markii dambe door ku lahayd ee Hana and Alice (2004).  Waxay ku guuleysatay abaalmarino badan jilitaankeeda. [ 1 ]
