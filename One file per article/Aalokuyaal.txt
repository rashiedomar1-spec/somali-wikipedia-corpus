Aalokuyaal [ 1 ] ama Calakuyaal (Magac Saynis: Senegalia caraniana (Chiov.) Kyal. & Boatwr. ) waa dhir oo laga heli karo wooqoyga bariga Soomaaliya iyo meel kale ma looga hubsano. [ 2 ] Geed dhuuban uu dhidhir ilaa 7 mitir leh uu yahay. Jilif cawlan iyo cadaan isku qasan wuu qabaa. Qodxo wuu leeyahay. [ 3 ] .
