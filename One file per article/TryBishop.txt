Trybishop ama TRYBISHOP wuxuu ku dhashay Jeremy Hicks oo kutaal &lt;a href="Saint%20Paul"&gt;Saint Paul&lt;/a&gt; gudaha &lt;a href="Minnesota"&gt;Minnesota&lt;/a&gt; ee dalka Maraykanka ahna [[Ameerikaan] qoobka ciyaariste, farshaxaniiste iyo soo saare kanasoo jeeda Minnesota.
Wuxuu sidhow ulashaqeeyay farshaxaniistayaal caan ah kuwaas oo xusid badan mudan. [[T-pain]], [[Jeezy]], [[Dolla]], 1500 ama Nothin, Da Internz iyo Curtis Williams.
Taariikh nololeedkiisa.
Wuxuu kudhashay Jeremy Hicks gudaha Saint Paul, [[Minnesota]].
Xiisayntii muusiga ee TryBishop waxay bilaabatay markii uu jiray lix sano. Xiisayntiisa muusigu uu sii kordhay sanadahaas sida uu usoosaaray muusig asaga ugaar ah.iyo lanashaqeeyay kuwabadan oo xusid mudan xaga qoobka ciyaarka, raabka iyo farshaxanka ee gudaha dalka Maraykanka.
Xirfadda.
Sanado badan kadib, Trybishop wuxuu soosaaray heeso kala duwan iyo bandhig farshaxano badan. (1)
Wax soosaarkiisa.
Trybishop wuxuu soosaaray heesta “Movin It” taasoo Tech N9ne, Beatnick, wrekoniz, iyo K-salaam. Iyo sidoo kale waa soosaare buuga sawirada (waa dhibaato) by Da' T.R.U.T.H.
Heesa kale oo uu soosaaray TRybishop waxaa ka mid ah heesta loogu talagalay Big K.R.L.T.
Bandhig.
Sanadkii 2012, Trybishop wuxuu soo bandhigay heesta by Christopher Dotson. iyo sidoo kale heestii “ thing now” oo leh T-pain. Intaas waxaa dheer, Trybishop wuxuu sidoo kale soo bandhigay farshaxano aadka looya qaan ee Dolla iyo Jeezy.
Trybishop wuxuu kaloo soo bandhigay heestii xayawaanka (shinbirihii) 2014 kii, sidoo kale wuxuu ka dhex muuqday buuga sawirada ee Curtis Williams.
Sanadkii 2015 kii, Trybishop oo kaashanaya EP ayaa soo bandhigay 1500 ama Nothin, Childish Major, Da’Internz iyo Prem Midha.
Inta badan dhawantii 2019. Trybishop wuxuu ka dhex muuqday buuga sawiradii Vernia oo leh Erick Sermon.
Tixraac.
