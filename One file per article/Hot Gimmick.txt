Hot Gimmick (ホットギミック, "Hotto Gimikku") waa &lt;a href="Manga"&gt;"shōjo" manga&lt;/a&gt; taxane qoran oo lagu muujiyey &lt;a href="Miki%20Aihara"&gt;Miki Aihara&lt;/a&gt;. Waxaa si taxane ah loogu daabacay majaladda "&lt;a href="Betsucomi"&gt;Betsucomi&lt;/a&gt;" ee &lt;a href="Shogakukan"&gt;Shogakukan&lt;/a&gt; laga soo bilaabo bishii Diseembar 2000 (oo la sii daayay bishii Nofembar) ilaa cadadkii Ogosto 2005 (la sii daayay bishii Luulyo). "Shogakukan" wuxuu uruuriyay &lt;a href="Tank%C5%8Dbon"&gt;cutubyada shaqsiyeedka&lt;/a&gt; 12 mug oo ku xidhan hoosta daabacaadda ubaxa Flower Comics. &lt;a href="Viz%20Media"&gt;Viz Media&lt;/a&gt; ruqsad u siiyay taxanaha sii-daynta luuqadda Ingiriiska ee Waqooyiga Ameerika.
"Hot Gimmick" wuxuu dhiirrigeliyay laba &lt;a href="Drama%20raadiyaha"&gt;Drama raadiyaha&lt;/a&gt; ah iyo &lt;a href="sheeko%20fudud"&gt;sheeko fudud&lt;/a&gt; kale oo dhammaad leh, oo tan dambe ay shati siisay Viz Media. La-qabsiga filimka tooska ah, oo cinwaan looga dhigay "" oo uu agaasime ka yahay &lt;a href="Y%C5%ABki%20Yamato"&gt;Yūki Yamato&lt;/a&gt;, ayaa laga sii daayay Jabaan Juun 28, 2019.
