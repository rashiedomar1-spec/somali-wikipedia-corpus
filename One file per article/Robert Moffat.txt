&lt;a href="Robert%20Moffat"&gt;Robert Moffat&lt;/a&gt; (21 Diseembar 1795 - 9 Agoosto 1883) wuxuu ahaa adeege Iskotish u diray &lt;a href="Afrika"&gt;Afrika&lt;/a&gt;, aabbihii Mary Moffat Livingstone iyo soddogga David Livingstone, iyo turjubaankii ugu horreeyay ee &lt;a href="Kitaabka%20quduska"&gt;Kitaabka Quduuska&lt;/a&gt; ah &lt;a href="Tswana"&gt;Tswana&lt;/a&gt;. Raadinta runta Moffat ayaa sidoo kale u horseeday inuu isticmaalo magaca Ilaah - Yehowah.
