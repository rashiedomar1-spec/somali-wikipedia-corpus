Degmooyinka Gobolka Raas Caseyr
Gobolka Raas-caseyr wuxuu ka koobanyahay 5 degmo oo kala ah "Caluula, Bareeeda, Baargaal, Murcaanyo iyo sido kale Gumbax".
Khayraadka Gobolka Raas-Caseyr
Gobolku wuxuu qani ku yahay khayraadka badda sida noocyada kala duwan ee kaluunka, Maydiga, faleenka, Timirta iyo sidoo kale Beeraha iyo Xoolaha nool.
Dadka dega Gobolka Raas-Caseyr
Gobolka raas-caseyr waxa daga 99% Beesha Harti gaar ahaan Beelaha &lt;a href="Siwaarqoon"&gt;Siwaakhroon&lt;/a&gt; (Majeerteen Harti) iyo Geesaguule Harti iyo Beesha tanade daarood ee leelkase kuwaas oo muddo qarniya ah ku sugnaa gobokaas.
