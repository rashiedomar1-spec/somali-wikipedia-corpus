title:
Duufaan (Hurricane Michael)

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=27214

text:
Hurricane Michael (2018) wuxuu ahaa duufaantii ugu xooganaa ee kulaylka loo yaqaan 'tropical cyclone' si uu u weeraro Maraykanka tan iyo Andrew sanadkii 1992 iyo kan ugu xoogga badan ee rikoodhka ku yaal Florida Panhandle. Waxay ahayd duufaantii sadexaad ee ugu weynaa ee ka dhasha dhulka Maraykanka iyadoo laga eegayo cadaadis, ka dambaysa 1935-kii Duufaanta Shaqaalaha iyo Hurricane Camille ee 1969, iyo sidoo kale duufaantii afaraad ee ugu xooganaa ee ka soo jeeda Maraykanka oo ka soo jeeda dabaysha xawaaraha.
