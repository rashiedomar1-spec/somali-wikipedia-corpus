Abron ama Bono ama Brong ama Bono Twi waa lahjad oo dadka Bono ku hadlaan. Waa la qiyaasaa inuu 1.2 milyan oo qof ku hadlaan Gaana , gaar ahaan gobolka Bono, iyo in ka badan 300,000 oo qoof ku nool Bariga Xeebta Foolmaroodi .
