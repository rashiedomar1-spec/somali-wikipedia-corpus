Kobernishiyaam
Kobernishiyaam (&lt;a href="ingiriis"&gt;ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;: Copernicium) astaanta (&lt;a href="Cn"&gt;Cn&lt;/a&gt;) waa &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiye&lt;/a&gt; kimiko oo tirada &lt;a href="atom"&gt;atom&lt;/a&gt;kiisu tahay boqol-toban-iyo-labo (112).
Curiyaha Kobernishiyaam waa &lt;a href="bir"&gt;bir&lt;/a&gt; ka tirsan &lt;a href="Transitional%20Metal"&gt;Transitional Metal&lt;/a&gt;ska ee &lt;a href="guruubka%2012"&gt;guruubka 12&lt;/a&gt;aad ee &lt;a href="Diwaanka%20Curiye"&gt;Diwaanka Curiye&lt;/a&gt; iyo guruubka &lt;a href="d-block"&gt;d-block&lt;/a&gt;. 
Curiyahan Kobernishiyaam waa &lt;a href="bir"&gt;bir&lt;/a&gt; radioactive ah oo lagu samayn karo laabka (laboratory) oo kali ah. Taas macnaheedu waa si dabiici ah looma heli karo curiyahan. 
Sidoo kale, &lt;a href="isotope"&gt;isotope&lt;/a&gt;ka kali ah ee dagan waa Kobernishiyaam-285 (285Cn) taasi oo leh cimri aad u yar, &lt;a href="ilbiriqsi"&gt;29 ilbiriqsi&lt;/a&gt;. 
Curiyahan waxaa markii ugu horeeysay la sameeyay sanadkii 1996dii xarun cilmi-baadhis oo ku taala dalka Jarmalka, waxayna ugu magac dareen xidigisyahan Nicolaus Copernicus.
