Who Wants to be a Millionaire? / Yaa Raba Inuu Noqdo Milyaneer? (oo mararka qaarkood loo yaqaan Milyaneer / Millionaire) waa bandhig kedis Maraykan ah oo ku salaysan bandhigga &lt;a href="Ingiriis"&gt;Ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;ka ee isla magaca. Mid ayaa ku guulaysan kara ilaa hal milyan oo &lt;a href="doolarka%20Maraykanka"&gt;doolarka Maraykanka&lt;/a&gt; ah ($1,000,000) bandhiga si uu uga jawaabo afar iyo toban (ama shan iyo toban nooc oo waaweyn) su'aalo aan kala go' lahayn oo isku xigta.
