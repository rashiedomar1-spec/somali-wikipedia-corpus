Caburinta ka dhanka ah Markhaatiyaasha Yehowah ee Poland sanadihii 1945–1956

Caburinta ka dhanka ah &lt;a href="https%3A//www.jw.org/so/"&gt;Markhaatiyaasha Yehowah&lt;/a&gt; ee Poland sanadihii 1945–1956 - xarig iyo caburin ay Wasaaradda Amniga Dadweynaha ka codsatay Markhaatiyaasha Yehowah ee &lt;a href="Boland"&gt;Poland&lt;/a&gt;. Ujeeddada ficilku waxay ahayd in la baabi'iyo kooxdan diimeed ee Poland maamulada shuuciga ah. Mowjadda ugu weyn ee qabqabashadu waxay dhacday habeenka Juun 21-22, 1950. Guud ahaan, qiyaastii 5,000 oo qof ayaa la xiray. Marka loo eego tirakoobka dowladda, caburinta muddadan waxay ahayd inay saameyso 90% Markhaatiyaasha Yehowah.
Tan iyo 1989, Markhaatiyaasha Yehowah dr &lt;a href="Boland"&gt;Boland&lt;/a&gt; waxay haysteen xorriyad, 130,000 xubnood.
