title:
Habmaamuuska baaxadsooraha ah

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=32831

text:
Habmaamuus baaxadsooraha ah (ingiriis: convention / protocol) waa qaanuunka iyo wax qabadka ee caadi, iyo Soo-jireen ah; Caado waa xeer guud oo la isku raacay, la qeexay, ama la aqbalay, fikrado, caadooyin bulsheed, ama halbeegyo, inta badan qaab caado ah.
