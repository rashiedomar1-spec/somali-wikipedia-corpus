Ludwik S. Kinicki (wuxuu ku dhashay Ogosto 20, 1896 magaalada Czortków, wuxuu ku dhintay Diseembar 19, 1944 Mauthausen-Gusen (KL))-Dhaqdhaqaaqa diinta Poland, bangi, tifaftiraha guud ee labada toddobaad ee Nowy Dzień ee &lt;a href="Boland"&gt;Poland&lt;/a&gt;. Isaga oo ah mid kormeera hawlaha &lt;a href="https%3A//www.jw.org/so/"&gt;Markhaatiyaasha Yehowah&lt;/a&gt; ee Dawladdii Guud, wuxuu ahaa maxbuus xerada Mauthausen-Gusen Nazi.
