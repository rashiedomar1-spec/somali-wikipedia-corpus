Las Anod - 3000 Dawac iyo dilka * Daadka Khatumo '

Laascaanood oo ay ciidamada Somaliland soo galeen magaalada , dagaal-yahano ka soo jeeda beesha dhulbahate iyo beelaha kale ee tiro yar ayaa lagu laayey dagaal ay la galeen ciidamada Somaliland oo ay ku dhinteen ama ku dhaawacmeen tiro badan.
