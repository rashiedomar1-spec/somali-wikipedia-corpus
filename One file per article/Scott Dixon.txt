Scott Ronald Dixon (wuxuu ku dhashay &lt;a href="Brisbane%2C%20Australia"&gt;Brisbane, Australia&lt;/a&gt;, Luulyo 22, 1981), oo lagu naaneeso "The Iceman", waa xirfadle ku takhasusay baabuurta &lt;a href="New%20Zealand"&gt;New Zealand&lt;/a&gt; ee hadda ku tartamaya &lt;a href="IndyCar%20Series"&gt;IndyCar Series&lt;/a&gt;. Wuxuu ahaa horyaalkii xilli ciyaareedyadii 2003, 2008, 2013, 2015, 2018 iyo 2020 kooxda &lt;a href="Chip%20Ganassi%20Racing"&gt;Chip Ganassi Racing&lt;/a&gt; team.
