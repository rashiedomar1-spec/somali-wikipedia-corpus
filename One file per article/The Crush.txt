The Crush

text:
The Crush waa filim Maraykan ah oo uu qoray oo uu agaasime ka yahay Alan Shapiro, kaas oo ay ku matasho Cary Elwes sida Nick Eliot iyo Alicia Silverstone sida Adrian Forrester, filimkeedii ugu horreeyay. Markii qoraaga 28-jirka ah ee Nick Elliot uu u guuro magaalo cusub, Adrian Forrester, gabadha 14-jirka ah ee mulkiilayaasha gurigooda uu kiraysto, isla markiiba waxay ka dhigaysaa soo jiidashada Nick. Si kastaba ha ahaatee, halka Nick uu isku dayayo in uu xiriir la yeesho sawir qaade Amy Maddik goobta shaqada, waxa uu dhibaato kala kulmayaa horumarinta Adrian. Marka uu dareenka Nick uu sii kordho, Adrian way ka cadhoonaysaa in loo diiday oo waxay bilawday inay weerarto hantidiisa iyo saaxiibadiisa. [ 1 ]
