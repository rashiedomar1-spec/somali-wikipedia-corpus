Super Cub (スーパーカブ, Sūpā Kabu ) waa taxane sheeko fudud oo Jabaaniis ah, oo uu qoray Tone Kōken oo uu tusay Hiro. Kadokawa Shoten ayaa sii daayay toddobo jumladood tan iyo bishii Maajo 2017 iyagoo ku hoos jira sumadooda Kadokawa Sneaker Bunko. La-qabsiga manga ee farshaxanka Kanitan ayaa si taxane ah looga sii daayey internetka tan iyo Diseembar 2017 iyada oo loo sii marayo mareegaha manga Comic Newtype ee Kadokawa Shoten.  Waxaa lagu soo ururiyay shan cabbir oo taangi.

La-qabsiga taxanaha telefishanka anime ee Studio Kai ayaa laga sii daayay Abriil 7 illaa Juun 23, 2021 shabakadaha telefishanka ee Japan oo dhan iyo Funimation -ka caalamiga ah. Taxanaha anime wuxuu helay dib u eegisyo togan.
