title:
Sunni-ururin

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=41823

text:
Sunni-ururin (af-ingiriis: frown, frowning) waa dareen wejiga ka muuqda oo badanaa ay ku jirto muruqyada indhaha oo isku biirsan iyo geesaha afka oo hoos u dhaca. Tani guud ahaan waxay muujineysaa dareen sida niyad-jab, xanaaq, walwal, ama xitaa jahwareer. Ficilkaasi wuxuu inta badan ka dhashaa jawaab celin aan toos ahayn oo ku saabsan dareen xun ama fikradaha.
