&lt;a href="Dhulgariir%20ka%20dhacay%20Ikwadoor"&gt;Dhulgariir ka dhacay Ikwadoor&lt;/a&gt; - &lt;a href="Dhulgariir"&gt;Dhulgariir&lt;/a&gt; cabirkiisu yahay 7.8 ayaa ku dhuftay Xeebta Baasifigga &lt;a href="Ikwadoor"&gt;Ikwadoor&lt;/a&gt; 16-kii Abriil, &lt;a href="2016"&gt;2016&lt;/a&gt;. Dhul-gariirka iyo dhul-gariirkii xigay ayaa geystay khasaare ballaaran waxaana ku dhintay in ka badan 650. &lt;a href="https%3A//www.jw.org/so/"&gt;Markhaatiyaasha Yehowah&lt;/a&gt; waxa ay u direen gawaari ay ku jiraan cunto iyo biyo la cabbo ah oo ay geeyeen aagga ay dhibaatadu ka dhacday.
