Kwon Yu-ri (dhashay Diseembar 5, 1989), oo ay sifiican u garaneyso magaceeda Yuri , waa heesaa Kuuriyada Koonfureed, jilaa.  Marka laga reebo howlaha kooxdeeda, waxay ka shaqeysay dhowr telefishan sida Fashion King (2012), Local Hero (2016), Gogh, The Starry Night (2016) iyo Innocent Defendant (2017). Sanadkii 2013, ayey filimkeedii ugu horreeyay ka soo muuqatay No Breathing .

2018, waxay sameysay kulankeedii ugu horeeyay ee soloist iyada oo leh EP-da ugu horeysa The First Scene .
