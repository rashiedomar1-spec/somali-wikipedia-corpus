Faxfax (cunto)

text:
"Kani waa maqaal ku saabsan maraqa. Haku khaldin &lt;a href="suugo"&gt;suugada&lt;/a&gt;.
"Halkan waxaa laga soo toosiyay boga "&lt;a href="faxfax"&gt;faxfax&lt;/a&gt;"
Faxfax (; ) waa &lt;a href="dareere"&gt;dareere&lt;/a&gt; ka mid ah &lt;a href="cunto"&gt;cuntada&lt;/a&gt; dadka taasi oo ka samaysan &lt;a href="biyo"&gt;biyo&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="khudaar"&gt;khudaar&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="midho"&gt;midho&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="hilib"&gt;hilib&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="xawaash"&gt;xawaash&lt;/a&gt; iyo noocyo kale oo geedo ah.
Faxfaxu waa cunto la diyaariyo, waxa sida badan laga sameeyaa iskudarka biyo, khudaar (&lt;a href="barandho"&gt;barandho&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="basal"&gt;basal&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="tamaandho"&gt;tamaandho&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="kaabaj"&gt;kaabaj&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="toon"&gt;toon&lt;/a&gt;ta iwm), hilib (sida &lt;a href="xoolo"&gt;hilibka xoolaha&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="digaag"&gt;digaag&lt;/a&gt;a iyo &lt;a href="kaluun"&gt;kaluun&lt;/a&gt;ka), xawaash iyo noocyo kale oo midho iyo khudaar ah. Faxfaxa waxaa lagu kariyaa &lt;a href="dheri"&gt;dheri&lt;/a&gt; ama digsi si loo helo dhadhan fiican wuxuu qaataa xoogaa wakhti ah.
Sida caadiga ah, faxfaxa waxaa loo qeybiyaa labo nooc: "faxfax fudud" ama "maraq fudud"; iyo "faxfax culus" ama "maraq culus". Maraqa fudud waa mid ka sameeysan biyo iyo xoogaa yar oo khudaar ah, isla markaana qaata wakhti aad u kooban. Halka maraqa culus ka sameeysan yahay hilib, khudaar badan iyo xawaash kala duwan. 
Maraqa kaligiis waa la cabi karaa, sidoo kale waxaa lagu darsadaa cuntada. Marar badan waxaa lagu cunaa &lt;a href="rooti"&gt;rooti&lt;/a&gt;da, &lt;a href="canjeero"&gt;canjeero&lt;/a&gt;da, &lt;a href="sabaayad"&gt;sabaayad&lt;/a&gt;a iyo noocyada kale ee cuntada ah.
