Kaasiyaam
Haku khaldin &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiyaha&lt;/a&gt; &lt;a href="Kaalshiyaam"&gt;Kaalshiyaam&lt;/a&gt;. 
Kaasiyaam (&lt;a href="ingiriis"&gt;ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;: Caesium) astaanta (&lt;a href="Cs"&gt;Cs&lt;/a&gt;) waa &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiye&lt;/a&gt; kimiko oo tirada &lt;a href="atom"&gt;atom&lt;/a&gt;kiisu tahay kontan-iyo-shan(55).
Curiyaha Aarsenik waa &lt;a href="bir"&gt;bir&lt;/a&gt; ka tirsan curiyayaasha &lt;a href="guruubka%201"&gt;guruubka 1&lt;/a&gt;aad, ([[Birta Alkali]), ee [[Diwaanka Curiye]]. 
Kaasiyaam waa [[bir]], midabka [[dahab]]ka oo iftiimaya leh, ka tirsan [[birta alkali]], isla markaana dareere noqota marka [[heerkul]]ku gaadho [[salsiyas|28 °C]] (82 °F) .
Si la mid ah [[Sodhiyaam]]ta iyo [[Botashiyaam]]ta, curiyaha Kaalsiyaam waa mid aad u firfircoon oo si dhakhso badan ula falgasha [[hawo|hawada]], [[biyo|biyaha]] iyo curiyeyaasha kale. 
Markii u horeeysay curiyahan waxaa helay labo [[saynis]]yahan ee kala ah Robert Bunsen iyo Gustav Kirchhoff [[sanad|sanadkii 1860]].
