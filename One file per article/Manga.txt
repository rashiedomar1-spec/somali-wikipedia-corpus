Manga ( Jabaan : 漫画 [maŋga] ) [ lower-alpha 1 ] waa sheekooyin sawir leh ama sheekooyin garaafyo ah oo ka yimid Jabaan. Inta badan manga waxay u hoggaansamaan qaab lagu soo saaray Japan dabayaaqadii qarnigii 19aad, [ 1 ] foomkuna wuxuu leeyahay taariikh hore oo ku saabsan fanka hore ee Jabaan . [ 2 ] Ereyga manga waxaa loo adeegsadaa Japan si loogu tixraaco  sheekooyin sawir leh iyo farshaxan kartoon . Ka baxsan Jabaan, erayga waxaa caadi ahaan loo isticmaalaa in lagu tilmaamo sheekooyin sawir leh markii hore lagu daabacay dalka.
