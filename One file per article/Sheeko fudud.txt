Sheeko fudud (ライトノベル, "raito noberu") waa qaab Jabaan ah &lt;a href="Mala-awaal%20dhalinyarada%20qaangaarka%20ah"&gt;qaangaar ah&lt;/a&gt; &lt;a href="Sheeko%20cusub"&gt;sheeko&lt;/a&gt; gaar ahaan bartilmaameedka ardayda &lt;a href="Dugsi%20sare"&gt;dugsiga sare&lt;/a&gt; iyo kuwa &lt;a href="Dugsi%20dhexe"&gt;dugsiga dhexe&lt;/a&gt;. Ereyga "sheeko fudud" waa &lt;a href="Wasei-eigo"&gt;"wasei-eigo"&lt;/a&gt;, ama erayga Jabaaniis oo laga sameeyay ereyada luqadda Ingiriiska.  Riwaayado fudud ayaa badanaa loogu yeeraa ranobe, ラノベ ama, Ingiriiska, LN.
