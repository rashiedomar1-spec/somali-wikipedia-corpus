Fasil Kenema Sport Club ( Amxaari : Fasil Keenma Sport Club), oo sidoo kale loo yaqaan Fasil City , waa naadi kubbadda cagta Itoobiya oo xirfad leh oo fadhigeedu yahay Gondar, Gobolka Axmaarada . Waxa ay ka ciyaaraan horyaalka Itoobiya oo ah heerka ugu sareeya ee kubbada cagta Itoobiya.
