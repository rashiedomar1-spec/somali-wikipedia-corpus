Kosofo ama Jamhuuriyadda Kosofo , ( Af-Albanian : Republika e Kosovës; Af-Serbian : Република Косово). Xilligan, Jamhuuriyadda Kosovo waxaa aqoonsan 93 ka mid ah 193 xubnood oo UN ka ah. [ 12 ] [ 13 ] [ 14 ] Dowladda Serbia uma aqoonsana Kosovo inay tahay dowlad madaxbanaan, laakiin waxay bilowday xiriir caadi ah oo ay la laheyd dowlada Kosovo , si kastaba ha noqotee, si waafaqsan Heshiiska Brussels . Heshiiska ayaa la soo gabagabeeyey 19 Abriil 2013. [ 15 ]
