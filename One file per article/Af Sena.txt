# Infobox

- **Dhalad u ah**: Musanbiig , Malaawi , Simbaabwi
- **Qoomiyada**: Sena
- **U ah afka hooyo**: 2,869,000  (2017–2020)
- **Qoyska luqada**: Niger–Congo Atlaantik–Kongo Benue–CKngo Bantoid Koonfur Bantu Nyasa Sena (N40) ? Af Sena
- **Dialects**: Rue (Barwe) Podzo
- **Ka ah Luqad rasmi**: Simbaabwi ('Chibarwe' loo yaqqano)
- **ka ah luuqadaha la aqoonsaday ee laga tirada badan yahay**: Malaawi
- **ISO 639-3**: Variously: seh – Mozambiquean Sena swk – Malawian Sena bwg – Barwe
- **Luqadda la adeegsado**: 99-AUS-xi ; also 99-AUS-xj (Chi-Rue), 99-AUS-xk (Gombe), 99-AUS-xl (Sangwe), & 99-AUS-xm (Chi-Podzo)
- **Guthrie code**: N.44,441 (N.45,46) [ 1 ]
