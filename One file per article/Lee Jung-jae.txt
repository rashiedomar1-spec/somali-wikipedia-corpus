Lee Jung-jae ( Kuuriya : 이정재 ; dhashay Diseembar 15, 1972 ) [ 1 ] waa atoore reer Koonfur Kuuriya ah iyo moodel hore. Wuxuu kasbaday dareenka adduunka oo dhan doorkiisa Seong Gi-hun ee taxanaha Netflix Squid Game .

Nolol

Bishii Janaayo 2015, Lee wuxuu xaqiijiyay inuu shukaansanayay Im Se-ryung. [ 2 ] Lee wuxuu hore u shukaansaday Kim Min-hee . [ 3 ]
