Showtime waa kanaalka fiilada &lt;a href="Gobolada%20Midoobay"&gt;Mareykanka&lt;/a&gt; ee ay iska leedahay &lt;a href="Paramount%20Global"&gt;Paramount Global&lt;/a&gt;. Barnaamijyada kanaalka waxaa ka mid ah taxane telefishan oo asal ah oo si gaar ah loogu soo saaray shabakadda tooska ah oo loo sameeyay adeegga la wadaago ee &lt;a href="Paramount%2B"&gt;Paramount+&lt;/a&gt; adeegga streaming, tiyaatarada la sii daayay iyo sawirro madax-bannaan, dukumentiyo, iyo majaajillo marmar ah oo taagan, filimada TV-ga loo sameeyay, iyo barnaamijyada dadka waaweyn ee softcore.
