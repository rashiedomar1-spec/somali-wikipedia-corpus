title:
Daraakoola

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=38172

text:
Filaad sedexaad oo loo yaqaan (Vlad the Impaler) ama "Daraakoola" wuxuu ahaa hogaamiyhaa gobolka Aflaaq waxa uuna noolaa (1431-1476), waxa uu ku caan baxay adeegsigiisa khaazuuqa iyo sida arxan darada leh uu u cadibi jiray cadawgiisa dadka uu ka qabto, waxa uu mudo dheer dagaaalo kula jiray &lt;a href="Dawlada%20Cosmaniya"&gt;Cusmaaniyiinta&lt;/a&gt; ilaa markii dambe ay ka dileen.
