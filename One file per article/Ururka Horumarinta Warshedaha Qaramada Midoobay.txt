Ururka Horumarinta Wershadaha Qaramada Midoobay ( Af Ingiriis : United Nations Industrial Development Organization , loo soo gaabiyo: ( UNIDO ); Af Carabi : لاسعانود ‎) waa wakiil khaas ah ee Qaramada Midoobay taasi oo degan magaalada Fiyeena, Austriya . Hadafka koowaad ee ururku waa horumarinta, talobixinta, dhiirigelinta iyo caawinta wershadaha .
Ururkani wuxuu xubin buuxda ka yahay Kooxda Horumarka Qaramada Midoobay . [ 1 ]
