Angela Gabrielle White waa atirisho filim qaawan oo Australiyaan ah isla markaana filim sameeye ah. [ 1 ] [ 2 ]

Waxbarasho

White wuxuu ka qalin jabiyay Jaamacadda Melbourne 2010 [ 3 ] oo uu ku qaatay darajada koowaad ee maamuuska ee daraasaadka jinsiga.

Shaqo

Angela White (magaceeda dhabta ah) [ 4 ] waxay shaqadeeda ka bilawday warshadaha qaangaarka sanadkii 2003. [ 5 ] Muuqaalkeedii ugu horeeyay waxaa toogtay Score wax yar uun markay 18 jirsatay. [ 5 ]

Sannadkii 2007, White waxaa loo magacaabay Model-ka Sannadka.

White waxay samaysay muuqaalkeedii ugu horreeyay ee galmo iyada iyo wiil 2011 dii DVD ah Angela White Finally Fucks . [ 5 ]

Sannadkii 2013, White ayaa billowday degelkeeda rasmiga ah, angelawhite.com. [ 6 ] Dabayaaqadii bishaas, White iyo Jessa Rhodes waxaa loo magacaabay Hablaha Rasmiga ah ee Abaal Marinta Abaalmarinta 2014 XBIZ. [ 7 ]

Muuqaalka warbaahinta kale

Sannadkii 2007, White waxay markii ugu horreysay ka sameysay telefishanka taxanaha majaajillada Australiya ee Pizza . [ 8 ]

Bishii Sebtember 2013, White ayaa ka soo muuqatay barnaamijka wararka Australia ee The Feed . [ 9 ]

Bishii Nofeembar 2013, Caddaan ayaa ka soo muuqday dokumenteriga The Vagina Diaries . [ 10 ]

Bishii Maajo 2015, White waxay ahayd mawduucyada dukumintiga gaaban The Porn Identity . [ 11 ]

2019, goos gooska iyada ayaa lagu soo bandhigay tijaabada taxanaha majaajillada HBO Mrs. Fletcher . [ 12 ]
