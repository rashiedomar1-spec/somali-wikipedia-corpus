GMA Network, Inc. (GMA, soo gaabinta "Global Media Arts" ah) waa xarun warbaahineed &lt;a href="Filibiin"&gt;Filibiin&lt;/a&gt; oo xarunteedu tahay &lt;a href="Quezon%20City"&gt;Quezon City&lt;/a&gt;. Waxaa la aasaasay 1950-kii &lt;a href="Robert%20La%20Rue%20Stewart"&gt;Robert La Rue Stewart&lt;/a&gt;.
Shirkaddu waxay ka shaqeysaa dhowr idaacadood iyo telefishin oo Filibiin ah, oo ay ku jiraan GMA-7, GTV-27, Super Radyo DZBB 594 iyo Barangay LS 97.1.
