Margot Elise Robbie ( ; 2 Luuliyo 1990) waa atariisho iyo soo saare Australian ah. Waxay heshay abaal-marinno kala duwan intii ay ku jirtay shaqadeeda, oo ay ku jiraan magacaabista laba &lt;a href="Abaalmarinta%20Akadeemiyada"&gt;Abaalmarinnada Akadeemiyada&lt;/a&gt;, saddex &lt;a href="Abaalmarinta%20Golden%20Globe"&gt;Abaalmarinnada Golden Globe&lt;/a&gt;, shan &lt;a href="Abaalmarinta%20Jilayaasha%20Shaashada"&gt;Abaalmarinta Jilayaasha Shaashada&lt;/a&gt; iyo shan &lt;a href="Abaalmarinta%20Filimada%20Akadeemiyada%20Ingiriiska"&gt;Abaalmarinta Filimada Akadeemiyada Ingiriiska&lt;/a&gt;. Majaladda "&lt;a href="Time"&gt;Time&lt;/a&gt;" ayaa sanadkan 2017 ku tilmaantay mid ka mid ah liiska &lt;a href="Time%20100"&gt;100-Ka Qof ee Ugu Saameynta Badan Caalamka&lt;/a&gt;, waxaana "&lt;a href="Forbes"&gt;Forbes&lt;/a&gt;" ay ku tilmaantay mid ka mid ah atariishooyinka ugu mushaarka badan adduunka ee 2019.
