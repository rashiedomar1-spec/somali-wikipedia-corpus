Damini Ebunoluwa Ogul(wuxuu dhashay 2 Luulyo 1991) xirfad ahaan loo yaqaan Burna Boy, waa heesaa reer Nayjeeriya ah, gabayaa ah, jilaa iyo soo saare rikoor ah. [ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] Waxa uu aqoonsi helay 2012 ka dib markii uu sii daayay "Like to party", Hogaamiyihii kalidii albamkiisii ugu horeeyay ee istuudiyaha L.I.F.E (2013). 2017, Burna Boy wuxuu heshiis rikoor ah la saxeexday Records Atlantic iyo shirkaddeeda waalid ee Warner Music Group. Albumkiisii saddexaad ee istuudiyaha ka baxsan, kaas oo sidoo kale u adeega sumaddiisa ugu horreysa, ayaa la sii daayay 2018. [ 4 ]
