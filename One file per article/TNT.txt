TNT (Turner Network Television) waa kanaalka fiilada &lt;a href="Gobolada%20Midoobay"&gt;Mareykanka&lt;/a&gt; ee uu leeyahay &lt;a href="Warner%20Bros.%20Discovery"&gt;Warner Bros. Discovery&lt;/a&gt;. Waxaa la bilaabay Oktoobar 3, 1988. Ujeedada asalka ah ee TNT waxay ahayd in la sii daayo aflaanta qadiimiga ah iyo taxane telefishan kuwaas oo Nidaamka Warbaahineed ee Turner uu ilaalinayo xuquuqda qulqulka iyada oo loo marayo kanaalka walaasha &lt;a href="TBS"&gt;TBS&lt;/a&gt;.
