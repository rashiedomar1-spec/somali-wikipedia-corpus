Laura Jeanne Reese Witherspoon (Maarso 22, 1976) waa atariisho Mareykan ah, soo saare, iyo ganacsade. Qofka helay abaal-marinno kala duwan, oo ay ku jiraan Abaalmarinta Akadeemiyada , laba Abaalmarinta Golden Globe , Abaalmarinta Filimka Akademiyadda Ingiriiska , Abaalmarinta Jilayaasha Shaashada , iyo Abaalmarinta Primetime Emmy , waa mid ka mid ah jilayaasha ugu mushaarka badan adduunka illaa 2019.
