Salidka Yurub

saliida yurub oo ruushka ay ku leedahay kaydka ugu badan ee wadamada norway iyo uk ka dib ruushka oo la xaqiijiyay kaydka saliida ceyriin , uk waxa ay ka heleen beero badan oo ku yaala waqooyiga badda iyo norway oo leh in ka badan 8 bilyan oo barrle oo saliid ah , wadamo badan oo yurub ah ayaa leh shidaal aad u firfircoon. saliida sida spain, turkiga, iyo talyaaniga
