title:
Booyad

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=4750

text:
Booyad waa baabuur loo isticmaalo, waxyaabaha Biyaha, Shidaalka, Gaaska ama waxyaabo kale oo warshadaha isticmaalaan.Booyad waxee ku socon kartaa biraha sida tareenka, ama baabuurta waaweyn sida baabuurta ina tareega ah. Booyadaha qaar waxee qaadaan waxyaabaha sida burka ama sonkorta.
