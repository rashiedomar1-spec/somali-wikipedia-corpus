Jilib

Jilibku uma eka shabbaha caadiga ah - in uusan ku foorarin hal dhidib. Dusha sare ayaa is -jiidata oo isku -beddesha marka la eego midba midka kale. Naqshaddan xariifka ahi waxay siisaa jilbaha dhaqdhaqaaqa ugu wanaagsan ee suurtogalka ah - waxay noo oggolaanaysaa inaan soconno, dheeshanno, barafaynno oo aan kaxaynno oo aan samaynno waxyaabo badan oo kale oo badan.
