The Secret Life of Marilyn Monroe

The Secret Life of Marilyn Monroe waa taxane telefishan-yar oo Mareykan ah oo loo sameeyay 2015 oo ku saabsan nolosha Marilyn Monroe . Waa Kelli Garner , Susan Sarandon , Emily Watson , Jeffrey Dean Morgan , iyo Eva Amurri Martino and was first aired on Lifetime on May 30 and 31, 2015. Taxanaha (laba-saac iyo afartan iyo toddoba daqiiqo) taxanaha ah wuxuu ku saleysan yahay buugga iibiyaha ugu wanaagsan ee New York Times oo uu qoray J. Randy Taraborrelli. Waxaa loo magacaabay sedex hal abuur Farshaxanka Abaalmarinta Emmy . [ 1 ]
