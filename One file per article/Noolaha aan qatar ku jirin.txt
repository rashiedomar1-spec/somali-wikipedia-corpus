Noolaha aan qatar ku jirin

Noolaha aan qatar ku jirin ( Af Ingiriis : least concern ; Af Carabi : لاسعانود ‎) waa koox noole ah kuwaasi oo Ururka Ilaalada Nolosha Dabiiciga ee Caalamka (International Union for Conservation of Nature) ku qiimeeyeen in aanay halis ama khatar ku jirin noloshooda iyo jiritaanka.
Sida caadiga ah, marnaba koox noole ah laguma dari karo xayndaabka noolaha aan khatarta ku jirin ilaa lagu sameeyo daraasad qoto dheer, taasi oo lagu ogaanayo bulshada noolahaas, tafiiranka iyo aayaha uu yeelan karo. [ 1 ]
