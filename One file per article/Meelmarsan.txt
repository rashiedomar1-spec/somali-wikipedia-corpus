text:
Meelmarsan (excellence) waa waxyaabaha oo heer sare ah.

Sareynta (meelmarsan) Ilaah - "sharafta" - "quruxda", "haybad", "sharafta".

Wanaagsanaantu waa karti ama tayo si aan caadi ahayn u wanaagsan oo ka sarraysa heerarka caadiga ah.

„Ilaahay baa kaamilnimo binuʼaadanka u soo celin doono.”
