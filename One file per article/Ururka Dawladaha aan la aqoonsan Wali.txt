Wadamada aan la aqoonsan iyo Rabitaanka Dadkeeda Af-Ingiriis Unrepresented Nations and Peoples Organization ama (UNPO) waa urur aan dawladi maamulin balse caalami ah. oo shucuubta kamidka ahi gabi ahaantood ay ku dhaqmaan Dimoqraadiyada . waxa ay kakoobanyihiin xubnaha ururkan kujiraa sida wadamada aan wali la ictiraafin ee goonida u taagan ama gobolada lahaysto ama sida gobolada dadkii aslku ulahaa lagu haysto. waxa kamida shuruudaha kuxidhan cidii rabta in ay kusoo biiraan shuruudahan soo socda.
