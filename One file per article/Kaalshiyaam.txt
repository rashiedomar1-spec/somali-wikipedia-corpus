Kaalshiyaam
Haku khaldin &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiye&lt;/a&gt;ha &lt;a href="Kaasiyaam"&gt;Kaasiyaam&lt;/a&gt; ee &lt;a href="Birta%20Alkali"&gt;Birta Alkali&lt;/a&gt;.
Kaalshiyaam (&lt;a href="Ingiriis"&gt;Ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;: Calcium) calaamad &lt;a href="Ca"&gt;Ca&lt;/a&gt; waa &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiye&lt;/a&gt; kimiko ah oo &lt;a href="tiro"&gt;tiro&lt;/a&gt;da &lt;a href="atom"&gt;atom&lt;/a&gt;kiisu tahay labaatan. 
Kaalshiyaam waa &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiye&lt;/a&gt; &lt;a href="bir"&gt;bir&lt;/a&gt; ka mid ah &lt;a href="guruubka%202"&gt;guruubka 2&lt;/a&gt;aad ee loo yaqaano &lt;a href="Birta%20Alkalineta%20Dhulka"&gt;Birta Alkalineta Dhulka&lt;/a&gt;, sidoo kale waa &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiye&lt;/a&gt;ha &lt;a href="shan"&gt;shan&lt;/a&gt;aad ee ugu jilicsan &lt;a href="Diwaanka%20Curiye"&gt;Diwaanka Curiye&lt;/a&gt;ha. 
Kaalshiyaamtu muhiim ayay u tahay &lt;a href="noole"&gt;noole&lt;/a&gt;ha ayadoo ka qeeyb qaadata dhismaha jidhka iyo lafaha.
