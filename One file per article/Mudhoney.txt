Mudhoney waa filimkii 1965 ee uu qoray Russ Meyer kuna saleysnaa sheeko-dariiqyada Streets Paved With Gold ee Raymond Friday Locke. Filimku waa riwaayad muddo la dejiyey xilligii Murugada Weyn.
