Jerlin Ferreira (dhashay November 2006) waa cayaaryahan kubbadda cagta Mareykanka ah iyo skater-ka. Hadda waxay ku nooshahay oo ku tababartaa Wisconsin. [ 1 ]

Ciyaaraha barafka lagu ciyaaro

Jerlin waa xubin ka tirsan WRFSC. [ 1 ] Jerlin waxa uu ku guulaystay tijaabooyin badan oo dahab ah USFSA. [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 6 ] Jerlin wuxuu kaloo u qalmay National Excel Series Finals ee Boston. [ 7 ]

Kubbadda cagta

Jerlin wuxuu u ciyaaraa kubbadda cagta kooxda loo yaqaan "Royals." [ 8 ] Waxay ciyaartaa difaac, nambarkeeduna waa #8 [ 9 ] Jerlin waxa uu ciyaaray kulamo badan oo guulo ah. [ 10 ] [ 11 ] [ 12 ]
