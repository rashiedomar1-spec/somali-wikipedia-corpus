Wolkite City FC ( ወልቂጤ ከተማ City FC , Wolkite Ketema ) waa naadi kubbadda cagta xirfad leh oo fadhigeedu yahay Wolkite, Itoobiya. Waxa ay ka ciyaaraan horyaalka Itoobiya oo ah heerka ugu sareeya ee kubbada cagta Itoobiya.
