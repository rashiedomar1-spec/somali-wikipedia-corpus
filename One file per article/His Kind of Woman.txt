His Kind of Woman ( Noociisa Naagtu ) waa Filim 1951, waxaana jilaya Robert Mitchum iyo Jane Russell . Filimku wuxuu ka kooban yahay bandhigyo taageeraya Vincent Price, Raymond Burr iyo Charles McGraw. Jihada filimka, oo salka ku haysay sheekada aan la daabicin ee "Star Sapphire" ee uu qoray Gerald Drayson, waxaa loo tirinayaa John Farrow. [ 1 ]
