Ahmet Kenan Evren (17 July 1917 - 9 May 2015) wuxuu ahaa siyaasi iyo sarkaal militari oo Turki ah, oo soo qabtay Madaxweynihii 7aad ee Turkiga 1980 ilaa 1989. Wuxuu qabtay jagada isagoo hogaaminaya afgembigii militariga ee 1980.
