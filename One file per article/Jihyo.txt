Park Ji-hyo (박지효; dhashay Park Ji-soo 1-dii Febraayo, 1997), oo loo yaqaan Jihyo , waa fanaanad Koonfur Kuuriya. Iyadu waa hogaamiyaha kooxda gabdhaha Twice oo hoos timaada JYP Entertainment . [ 1 ] [ 2 ]

Nolosha shaqsiyeed

Bishii Ogosto 2019, wakaaladaha matalaya Jihyo iyo Kang Daniel , oo hore xubin uga ahaa Wanna One iyo aasaasaha Konnect Entertainment, ayaa xaqiijiyay inay shukaansanayaan. [ 3 ] [ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 6 ] [ 7 ] Waxaa lagu tilmaamay inay yihiin "lammaane awood leh" oo cusub oo K-pop ah warbaahinta.
