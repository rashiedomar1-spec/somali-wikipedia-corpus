Mina Sharon Myoi (dhashay Maarso 24, 1997), [ 3 ] oo lagu naanayso Mina , waa fanaanad reer Jabaan ah oo ku dhalatay Maraykanka oo fadhigeedu yahay Koonfur Kuuriya. Waxay xubin ka tahay kooxda gabdhaha Twice , oo ay samaysay JYP Entertainment. Mina waa mid ka mid ah Twice saddexda xubnood ee Jaban. [ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 6 ]
