Gilda waa 1946 filim Mareykan ah oo ay jilaysay Rita Hayworth . [ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] Sannadkii 2013, filimka waxaa maktabadda Koongarasku u dooratay in lagu keydiyo Diiwaanka Filimada Qaranka ee Mareykanka isagoo "dhaqan ahaan, taariikh ahaan ama bilic ahaan muhiim u ah". [ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 6 ]
