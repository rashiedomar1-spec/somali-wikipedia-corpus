Dadka gaagaaban

text:
Dadka gaagaaban (af-ingiriis: midget ama dwarfism) waa qof ku dhashtay cudurka loo yaqaan achondroplasi oo dhirirkiisu ka yareenaayo 147 cm.
Dad waxaa jiro oo gaagaaban oo cudurkaas aan ku dhalan sida &lt;a href="dadka%20Pygmy"&gt;dadka Pygmy&lt;/a&gt; oo dherirkoodu ka yaryahay 150cm oo ku kala nool dalal dhowr ah oo aduunka ku yaal, gaar ahaan afrika..
