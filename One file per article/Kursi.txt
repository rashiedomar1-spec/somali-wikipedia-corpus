Kursi (; ) waa saabaan oo loo isticmaalo fadhisasho. Kursiga waxa la saara &lt;a href="futada"&gt;badhyaha&lt;/a&gt;.
Magaca.
Ereyga "kursi" waa mid asal ahaan ka soo jeeda &lt;a href="Af-Carabi"&gt;luuqada Carabiga&lt;/a&gt;. Si la mid ah ereyo badan oo kale, ereyga kursi waa mid laga soo amaahdey luuqada Carabiga.
