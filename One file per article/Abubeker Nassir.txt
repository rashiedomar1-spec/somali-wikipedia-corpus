Abubeker Nassir Ahmed (; waxa uu dhashay 23 Febraayo 2000) waa xirfadle &lt;a href="Kubadda%20Cagta"&gt;kubbadda cagta&lt;/a&gt; Itoobiya ah oo u ciyaara weerarka hore ee kooxda Mamelodi Sundowns ee Premier Soccer League iyo xulka qaranka Itoobiya . Waxa uu hore ugu soo ciyaaray kooxda Ethiopian Coffee SC ee horyaalka Ethiopia .
