title:
Lamaane-inko

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=40696

text:
Lamaane-inkeed ama inko-lamaane , oo af-ingiriis lagu dhaho significant other ( ingiriis : spouse ) waa mid ka mid ah laba qof oo is guursaday. Qofka inko-lamaaneed ah, ama lamaane-inkeed ah, diinta Islaamka daraadeed, waa qofka shuruudaha guurka qof kale la galay sida meherka o kale ama soo doonista soo afjaray. Xagga dadka mulxidka ah, lamaane-inkeedka wuxuu noqonaya qofka oo guri ay wada degaan iyo hawlaha haasaawaha ay ku maaweelaan. [ 1 ]
