Kooxda kubbada cagta qaranka ee Yugoslavia [ lower-alpha 1 ] Yugoslavia u matalay kubbada cagta xidhiidhka caalamiga ah.

In kasta oo kooxdu inta badan matalaysa Boqortooyada Yugoslavia ka hor dagaalka iyo SFR Yugoslavia ka dib, soo noqnoqoshada kala duwan ee gobolka ayaa si rasmi ah loogu aasaasay kubbadda cagta, oo ay ku jiraan:

Waxay ku riyaaqday guulo tartan caalami ah, iyadoo gaartay semi-finalka [ lower-alpha 2 ] ee 1930 iyo 1962 FIFA World Cups . Sannadkii 1992-kii, intii lagu jiray dagaalladii Yugoslavia, kooxda ayaa laga joojiyay tartanka caalamiga ah ee qayb ka ah cunaqabataynta Qaramada Midoobay ee Yugoslavia .
