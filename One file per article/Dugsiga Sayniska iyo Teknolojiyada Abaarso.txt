Dugsiga Sayniska iyo Teknolojiyada Abaarso ( Af Ingiriis : Abaarso School of Science and Technology ) (sidoo kale loo yaqaano Abaarso School ) waa dugsi iska-wax-u-qabso ah, ardeydu ku dhex nooshahay kaasi oo ku yaala tuulada Abaarso , Maroodi Jeex ee wadanka Somaliland .

Sanadkii 2014ka, Dugsiga Sayniska iyo Teknolojiyada Abaarso waxaa Ururka New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC) gudoonsiiyeen shahaado sharaf dugsigu ku mutaystay dadaalka iyo horumarinta adeyda. [ 1 ]
