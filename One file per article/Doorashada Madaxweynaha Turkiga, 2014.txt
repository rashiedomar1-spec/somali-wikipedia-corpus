Waxay dhacday 10 agoosto 2014 doorashada madaxtinimada si loo doorto Madaxweynaha Turkiga ee laba iyo tobanaad, waana markii ugu horreysay doorashada madaxweynaha jamhuuriyadda Turkiga Doorasho toos ah . [ 1 ] , natiijadiina waxay ku dhamaatay guusha raysulwasaarihii hore ee Turkiga Rajab Dayib Erdogaan isla wareegga koowaad, asigoo helay in ka badan 50% codadka cod bixiyeyaasha.
