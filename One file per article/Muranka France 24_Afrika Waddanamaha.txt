Muranka France 24/Afrika Waddanamaha
EN: Disputes with france 24
Burkina Faso.
Burkina faso iyo france 24 muran ku saabsan taageerada kooxaha argagixisada ee Burkina Faso
Somaliland.
Maqaal la qoray 2023 qaab muuqaal ah ayaa lagu sheegay in Somaliland ay ka maqan tahay maalgashi ay Somaliland maal-gashi badan ku samaynaysay sannadihii u dambeeyay iyo muranka dadka reer Somaliland ee ka jira dalka Faransiiska waxa kale oo ay BBC-da ka mamnuucday sababo la xidhiidha burburinta dawladnimada Somaliland ee bariga Afrika
