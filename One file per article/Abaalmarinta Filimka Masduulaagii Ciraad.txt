Abaalmarinta Filimka Masduulaagii Ciraad ( Hangul : 청룡영화상 ; RR : Cheongryong Yeonghwasang ) waa xaflad abaalmarin sanadle ah oo lagu soo bandhigo Sports Chosun (sumad walaashiis ah Chosun Ilbo ) si heer sare ah filimka Koonfur Kuuriya . [ 1 ]
