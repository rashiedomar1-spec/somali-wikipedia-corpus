title:
Inqilaab Ciidan

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=40298

text:
Inqilaab militari, oo sidoo kale loo yaqaan inqilaab, waxaa loola jeedaa awood dowladeed si lama filaan ah oo sharci darro ah ula wareego xoog militari, badanaa iyadoo loo adeegsado awood ama hanjabaad. Tallaabadan ayaa caadiyan looga dan leeyahay in lagu afgambiyo dowladda jirta ama hoggaanka siyaasadeed.
