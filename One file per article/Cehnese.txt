Luqadda Acehnese (Achinese) waa luqad Malayo-Polynesian ah oo ay ku hadlaan dadka Acehnese oo ku nool Aceh, Sumatra, Indonesia. Luqaddan waxaa sidoo kale ku hadasha faracyada Acehnese ee qaybo ka mid ah Malaysia sida Yan, ee Kedah.
