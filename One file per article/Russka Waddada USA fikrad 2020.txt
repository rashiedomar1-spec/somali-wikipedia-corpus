Russka Waddada USA fikrad &lt;a href="2020"&gt;2020&lt;/a&gt;
&lt;a href="https%3A//edition.cnn.com/travel/article/trans-siberian-road/index.html"&gt;CNN.com&lt;/a&gt;
Fikradda waddo ka timaadda ruushka oo aadda dhinaca woqooyiga Ameerika, halkaas oo xarumaha gaadiidka ay ka bixin doonaan alaabooyin dhaqso leh, ammaan badan, iyo gaadiid badan oo la geynayo labada ummadood ee Ruushka iyo Ameerika, motoway ayaa ka sarreyn doonta badda qaybo ka mid ah.
Motoway waxay noqon doontaa isbadal weyn oo dhanka gaadiidka ah ee gobolka nabada.
