Makaso.
Makaso" waa tuulo xeebeed dhacda bariga gobolka &lt;a href="Jubbada%20Dhexe"&gt;Jubada Dhexe&lt;/a&gt; ee dhinaca &lt;a href="badweynta%20Hindiya"&gt;badweynta Hindiya&lt;/a&gt; isla markaana ah Tuulo yar ay ku noolyihin tiro yar qoysas reer miyi ah."
Deegankan waxa loogu magac daray ceel gacmeedka la yiraahdo Makaso
Makaso waxa degta beesha sheekhaal gaar ahaan jufada &lt;a href="Samafale%20Caagane"&gt;Samafale Caagane&lt;/a&gt; Lobage.
