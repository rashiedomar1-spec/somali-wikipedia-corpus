BEESHA GARWAALE

Waa beel ka mid ah beelaha Digil iyo Mirifle ee loo yaqaanno RAXAWEYN(REEWING). Gaar ahaan beesha GARWAALE waxay ka tirsan tahay Mirifle.

Mirifle naftirkiisu wuxuu u sii kala baxaa sagaal iyo siddeed (9&8). Beesha GARWAALANA waxay ka mid tahay beelaha siddeedda.

Beesha GARWAALE waxay u kala baxdaa shan lafood oo kala ah:

1. GARDOON

2. MAANDOW

3. WAAGBIYE

4. RAARANGLE

5. MUGAAWLE
