Denisan (siyaasi)

text:
Dareenka Denisan ama jawi denisan ( Af Ingiriis : intransigence ama obstinate ; Af Carabi : لاسعانود ‎) dhanka siyaasada waa habsocod iyo qiiro ee taxna waqtiga kacdoonka White Movement ama Dhaqdhaqaaqa Cadaan , ee ahaa jabhad huwan ah ee lid ku ah dawladda Hantiwadaaga ama dawladda Hanti-wadareedka ee Ruushka sanadaha 1917 ilaa 1923. Ujeedada dareenka denisan ama jawiga denisan waxay ahayd in lagaga hor yimaado siyaasadda Joseph Stalin aa waqtigaas ahaa madaxweynaha dhaqdhaqaaqa Cadaan ee Ruushka. [ 1 ] [ 2 ]
