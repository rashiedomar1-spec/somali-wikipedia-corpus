Jaamacadda Maraykanka waa jaamacad gaar loo leeyahay, Jaamacadda Metodiis oo ku taal &lt;a href="Washington%2C%20D.C."&gt;Washington, D.C.&lt;/a&gt; Jaamacadda waxaa dhigta ilaa 9,940 arday. Waxaa la aasaasay 1893. Madaxweynaha hadda ee jaamacadda waa Xoghayihii hore ee Caafimaadka iyo Adeegyada Aadanaha ee &lt;a href="Maraykanka"&gt;Maraykanka&lt;/a&gt; Sylvia Mathews Burwell ilaa Juun 2017.
=Ciyaaraha=
Kooxaha ciyaaraha fudud ee jaamacada waxa lagu magacaabaa Eagles. Waxay leeyihiin 9 kooxood oo farsiiti ah waxayna ku tartamayaan Patriot League.
