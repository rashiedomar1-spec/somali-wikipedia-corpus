Sung Jae-ki ama Sung Jae-gi (Korea:성재기 / 成在基, 11 Sebteembar 1967 - 26 Luuliyo 2013 ) waa Human right activists ama Civil right activists, [ 1 ] Liberalinimo Filasoofiyaasha ee Koonfur Kuuriya . [ 2 ] Sannadkii 2008, aasaasaha ee Midowga urrurka sare ee Korean lab (남성연대 / 男性連帶).

In 2013, wuxuu ahaa sucide, [ 3 ] oo iska soo horjeeda oo takoor ah galmada.
