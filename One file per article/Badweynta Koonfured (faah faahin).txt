title:
Badweynta Koonfured (faah faahin)

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=33876

text:
Badweynta Koonfureed, oo sidoo kale loo yaqaan Badweynta Koonfureed ee Weyn, Badweynta Antarctic iyo Badweynta Antarctic, waxay ka kooban tahay biyaha koonfureed ee Badweynta Adduunka, guud ahaan waxay dabooshaa koonfurta 60 ° S loolka waxayna ku wareegsan tahay Antarctica ama Antarctica. shanta badeed ee waaweyn ..  Gobolkan badweynta waxaa loo tixgeliyaa qabow, maadaama biyaha woqooyi ay ka soo qulqulayaan Antarctica oo ay isku dhex qasan yihiin biyaha diirran ee gobolka subarctic.
