"Sidoo kale eeg: &lt;a href="Milyan"&gt;Milyan&lt;/a&gt;"
&lt;a href="Bilyan"&gt;Bilyan&lt;/a&gt; (&lt;a href="Ingiriis%20%28luqad%29"&gt;af-ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;: Billion, &lt;a href="Carabi"&gt;af-carabi&lt;/a&gt; مليار) waa tiro xisaabeed oo ka kooban toban god. Tusaale ahaan, hal Bilyan waa 1'000'000'000.
Tiroda Bilyan waxa ka sareeya ama ka badan Tiriliyan oo ka sameeysan sadex-iyo-toban god (hal tiriliyan = 1'000'000'000'000) waxaana ka hooseeya Bilyanka tirada loo yaqaano Milyan oo ah todoba god (hal milyan = 1'000'000).
