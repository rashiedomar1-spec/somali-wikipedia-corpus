Ururka Badaha Caalamiga ( Af Ingiriis : International Maritime Organization ; Af Carabi : لاسعانود ‎) (loo soo gaabiyo IMO ) wakhtiyadii hore loo yaqaanay Ururka Talobixinta Badaha Dowladaha (Inter-Governmental Maritime Consultative Organization; IMCO ) [ 3 ] waa wakiilka khaaska Qaramada Midoobay u qaabilsan socdaalka maraakiibta iyo gooshitaanka badweynta caalamka. Sanadkii 1948 ayaa magaalada Jeniifa lagu aasaasay ururka IMO [ 4 ] wuxuuna shaqo bilaabay toban sano ka dib, wakhtigaasi oo talaabada ugu horeeysay qaaday sanadkii 1959.
