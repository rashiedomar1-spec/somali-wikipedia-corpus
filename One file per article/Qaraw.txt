Qaraw (; &lt;a href="Af-Carabi"&gt;Af-Carabi&lt;/a&gt;: كابوس) waa riyo aan fiicnayn ama dhib badan.
Kuwo badan oo soo maray xasuuqii naxdinta lahaa ee dagaalka si fudud uma illoobi karaan, qaarna ma iloobi karaan dareenka ah in dambiile soo weeraro. Khibradaha noocan oo kale ah ee dhibta leh inta aan soo jeedno waxay ku soo bixi karaan riyooyinkeenna, waxayna keenaan qarow. Waxyaabaha caadiga ah ee maskaxdeena ku jira markaan seexanayno waxay ku soo bixi karaan riyooyinkeena.
&lt;a href="Waagii%20dhexe"&gt;Waagii dhexe&lt;/a&gt;, waxaa loo malaynayay in riyooyinka qarow ay sabab u yihiin &lt;a href="jinni"&gt;jinni&lt;/a&gt;yada oo fadhiya caloosha kuwa hurda.
