Jaamacadda Teknolojiyada Somaliland ( Af Ingiriis : Somaliland University of Technology ) ( SUTECH ) waa jaamacad dadweyne taasi oo ku taala magaalada Hargeysa , magaalo-madaxda u ah Jamhuuriyada Soomaaliland . [ 1 ]

# Infobox

- **La aas aasay**: 2000 (iskubedeshay heer jaamacadeed 2007)
- **Nuuca**: jaamacad dadweyne
- **Madaxwenaha Jaamacadda**: Dr. Saciid Sheekh Maxamed
- **Waxay ku taal**: Wadada Berbera, Geed Habeeya, Hargeysa
- **Dhismaha jaamacadda**: 20 hektar (49 acres)
- **Magacyadii hore**: Hargeisa College of Applied Arts & Technology
- **Midabuka**: Cagaar iyo oraanji
