Jeong Eun-ji (dhashay Jeong Hye-rim , ee Agoosto 18, 1993), oo si fiican loogu yaqaan Eunji , waa fanaanad u dhalatay Koonfur Kuuriya, curiyaha heesaha, atariisho, DJ raadiyaha, atariisho masrax, iyo jilaa cod. Waxay caan ku tahay xubin ka mid ah kooxda gabdhaha Koonfur Kuuriya Apink . Jeong waxay sameysay jilitaankeedii ugu horreeyay taxanaha soo socda da'da, Reply 1997 ee 2012. Waxay tan iyo markaas door ku lahayd That Winter, the Wind Blows (2013), Trot Lovers (2014), Cheer Up! (2015), and Untouchable (2017) marka lagu daro doorarka ku -simaha codka kala duwan.  Waxay soo saartay albumkeedii ugu horreeyay, Dream , ee 2016. [ 1 ]
