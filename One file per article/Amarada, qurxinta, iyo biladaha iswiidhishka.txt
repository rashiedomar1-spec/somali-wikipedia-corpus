Amarrada iswiidhishka- &lt;a href="Sweden"&gt;Sweden&lt;/a&gt;
Amarrada, qurxinta, iyo biladaha iswiidhishka waxay leeyihiin taariikh asal ah, iyagoo ku soo celinaya 1606 aasaasiga ah ee Amarka Yehova. Amarka royal Royal of Knights ee Sweden ayaa si dhab ah loo cayimay qarnigii 18aad, iyada oo aasaaskooda rasmiga ah ee 1748 by Frederick I ee Sweden. Dib-u-habeyn muhiim ah oo lagu sameeyay 1974 ayaa isbeddelay shuruudaha iyo shuruudaha ayadoo amarro badan iyo qurxinba la siin karo.
