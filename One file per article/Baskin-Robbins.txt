Baskin-Robbins waa shirkada ugu weyn ee soo saarta jalaatada taasi oo xarunteedu tahay Canton, Massachusetts . [ 2 ] Shirkadan waxaa aasaasay Burt Baskin iyo Irv Robbins sanadkii 1945kii.

Faalo

Baskin-Robbins taxane ah goobaha jalaato in uu yahay caalamiga ah ee dabiiciga ah, Burt Baskin iyo Irv Robbins aasaasay sanadkii 1953. daboolida Produced for jalaato Glendale, California. Shirkadda waa kala duwan oo takhasus ah oo inta badan dunida meelaha jalaato ah 5,800, 2.800 kaas oo faracyadii ahaayeen heli karaa Mareykanka oo waaweyn. Baskin Robbins leeyahay qaybinta jalaato in ka badan 30 dal oo dunida ah. Shirkadda xaruuntiisu tahay Canton, Massachusetts.
