Boom waa 2003 filim Hindi majaajillo madoow la sii daayay 19-kii Sebtember 2003. Waxay baartaa ku lug lahaanshaha adduunka moodada iyada oo la adeegsanayo dambiyada ka hooseeya adduunka.

Waxaa Director ka ah Kaizad Gustad waxaana soo saaray Jackie Shroff xaaskiisa, Ayesha Shroff, filimkaan waxaa kujira Amitabh Bachchan , Jackie Shroff , Gulshan Grover , Padma Lakshmi , Madhu Sapre , Zeenat Aman iyo Katrina Kaif . Boom waa Katrina Kaif filimkeeda ugu horreeya [ 1 ] .
