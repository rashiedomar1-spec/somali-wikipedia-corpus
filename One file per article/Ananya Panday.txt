Ananya Panday (dhashay 30 Oktoobar 1998) waa atirisho Hindi ah oo ka shaqeysa filimada &lt;a href="Bollywood"&gt;Hindiga&lt;/a&gt;.  Waa gabadha uu dhalay Aktarka &lt;a href="Chunky%20Pandey"&gt;Chunky Pandey&lt;/a&gt;.  Waxay doorkeedii ugu horeeyay ka qaadatay "&lt;a href="Student%20of%20the%20Year%202"&gt;Student of the Year 2&lt;/a&gt;" sanadka 2019, waxaa ku xigtay door "&lt;a href="Pati%20Patni%20Aur%20Woh"&gt;Pati Patni Aur Woh&lt;/a&gt;" isla sanadkaas. Dooryadaan waxay ku muteysteen &lt;a href="Abaalmarinta%20Filmfare%20oo%20Horeynta%20Dhedig%20ugu%20Fiican"&gt;Abaalmarinta Filmfare oo Horeynta Dhedig ugu Fiican&lt;/a&gt;.
Nolosha hore.
Panday waxay dhalatay 30 Oktoobar 1998. Aabaheed waa jilaa Chunky Pandey. Waxay wax ku baratay &lt;a href="Dugsiga%20Dhirubhai%20Ambani%20Caalamiga"&gt;Dugsiga Dhirubhai Ambani Caalamiga&lt;/a&gt; ilaa 2017. Waxay ka qayb qaadatay dhacdada "&lt;a href="Vanity%20Fair"&gt;Vanity Fair&lt;/a&gt;" Le &lt;a href="Bal%20des%20d%C3%A9butantes"&gt;Bal des débutantes&lt;/a&gt; ee &lt;a href="Baariis"&gt;Baariis&lt;/a&gt; 2017.
