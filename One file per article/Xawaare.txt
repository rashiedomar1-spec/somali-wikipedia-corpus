Xawaare (; ) waa &lt;a href="halbeeg"&gt;halbeeg&lt;/a&gt;ga lagu ogaado orodka awoodda socota ee shay, aalad ama walax ku socoto &lt;a href="wakhti"&gt;waqti&lt;/a&gt; kooban gudaheed. 
Si la mid ah &lt;a href="halbeeg"&gt;halbeeg&lt;/a&gt;yada &lt;a href="dherer"&gt;dherer&lt;/a&gt;ka, &lt;a href="balac"&gt;balac&lt;/a&gt;a iyo &lt;a href="joog"&gt;joog&lt;/a&gt;a ayaa masaafada xawaaraha loo qeybiyaa &lt;a href="wakhti"&gt;waqtiga&lt;/a&gt;. 
Astaanta &lt;a href="SI"&gt;Nidaamka Caalamiga ee Halbeega Dunida&lt;/a&gt; ee xawaaruhu waa &lt;a href="mitir"&gt;mitir/ilbiriqsi&lt;/a&gt; ama (&lt;a href="m/s"&gt;m/s&lt;/a&gt;). 
Sidoo kale waxaa la isticmaalaa &lt;a href="Kilomitir"&gt;kilomitir/saacad&lt;/a&gt; ama (km/h), iyo dalalka qaar sida &lt;a href="Maraykanka"&gt;Maraykanka&lt;/a&gt; waa &lt;a href="Mayl"&gt;mayl/saacad&lt;/a&gt; (mi/h). 
Inta la ogyahay, xawaaraha ugu dheereeya ee &lt;a href="awood"&gt;awood&lt;/a&gt; ama shay kale ku socon karo waa &lt;a href="Xawaare%20Ileys"&gt;Xawaaraha Ileyska&lt;/a&gt; kaas oo la mid ah 299,792,458 &lt;a href="mitir"&gt;mitir&lt;/a&gt; &lt;a href="ilbiriqsi"&gt;ilbiriqsi&lt;/a&gt;giiba (m/s), taas oo qiyaas ahaan u dhiganta 1079 &lt;a href="milyan"&gt;milyan&lt;/a&gt;
&lt;a href="kilomitir"&gt;kilomitir&lt;/a&gt; saacadiiba ama (671,000,000 mph).
