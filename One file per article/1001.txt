1001 (MI) wuxuu ahaa sannad caadi ah oo bilaabmaya Arbacada ee kalandarka Luuliyaan, sanadka 1001aad ee Casriga (CE) iyo Anno Domini (AD), sannadkii 1aad ee millennium 2aad, sannadkii 1aad ee qarnigii 11aad.  iyo sannadkii 2aad ee 1000-kii tobankii sano ee la soo dhaafay.
