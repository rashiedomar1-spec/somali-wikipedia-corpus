Biography.
Aisha was born in Suwon, South Korea. She began her career as a member of Everglow, a girl group that debuted in 2019. Aisha is a former entertainment coach at JYP Entertainment. She got her stage name "Aisha" from the Korean word for "Asia", which means "[she will be] an idol who succeeds in Asia".
