Sakurako Konishi (小 西 桜 子 Konishi Sakurako ) (wuxuu dhashay 29 Maarso 1998) waa atirisho Jabaan ah oo reer Saitama ah. [ 1 ]

Shaqo

Konishi wuxuu markii ugu horeysay xiiseynayay xirfad jilista kadib markii uu daawaday filimka Sion Sono ee Himizu ee dugsiga sare ee yar. [ 2 ] Waxay markii dambe ka soo muuqatay filim iskeed u soo saartay jaamacadda, waxayna bilowday inay dib ugu dirto resumeygeeda wakaaladaha madadaalada isla markaana ay ka soo qeyb gasho dhageysiga.  Kadib markii ay la kulantay agaasimaha filimka Masaoki Hirota, waxaa loo jilay halyeeyga filimkiisii ​​2020 ee Sasaki In My Mind , kaas oo noqday filimkeedii ugu horreeyay ee ganacsi. [ 2 ] Waxay ku jileysay Filimka Takashi Miike ee First Love , waxaa laga xushay 3,000 oo gabdhood doorka 2019. [ 3 ] Waxay sidoo kale ka soo muuqatay jeldiga majaladda Young King , ee daabacaadii 2 Maarso 2020, oo uu daabacay Shōnen Gahōsha. [ 4 ]
