title:
Doob qorshayn

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=22288

text:
Doob qorshayn ama doob ahaansho (ingiriis: "involuntary celibate" ama "incel", iyo "incelhood", iyo "inceldom") waa qofka guur-la'aan iyo aan lahayn fahanka ama xirfadaha shukaansiga, isaga ikhtiyaarka lahayn. Sababta xaaladaas dhacdo waxay noqon karta lacag la'aanta, xishoodka xad-dhaaf ah ama &lt;a href="foolxunimo"&gt;foolxunimo&lt;/a&gt;.
