Hinjinne u sara kaca&lt;/br&gt;
Calankaan harraad iyo &lt;/br&gt;
Haydaar u mudateen. &lt;/br&gt;
Hir cagaarku qariyayiyo &lt;/br&gt;
Habkay samadu tahayoo &lt;/br&gt;
Xiddig dhi igleh hoorshoo &lt;/br&gt;
Caddaan lagu hadheeyaay. &lt;/br&gt;
Maxaa haybad kugu yaal!&lt;/br&gt;
