Asmaa bin Abiiba kar Gabar saxaabiyad ah uu dhalay &lt;a href="Abu%20Bakr"&gt;Abuu bakar&lt;/a&gt; ahna walaasha ka weyn &lt;a href="Caa%27isha%20binti%20Abiibakar"&gt;Caa'isha&lt;/a&gt; waxaa guursaday &lt;a href="Zubeyr%20bin%20Cawaam%20R.C"&gt;Zubeyr bin Cawaam&lt;/a&gt; oo kamid ahaa &lt;a href="10-ka%20saxaabi%20ee%20Janada%20loogu%20bishaareeyay"&gt;Tobankii janad loogu bishaareeyey&lt;/a&gt; . Waxaa loogu yeeri jiray "Daatu nidaaqeyn" Maxaa yeelay waxey jeexday maradeyd ayadoo ugu xirtay alaabta ay wateen Nabi &lt;a href="Nebi%20Maxamed%20c.s"&gt;Muxamad scw&lt;/a&gt; iyo &lt;a href="Abu%20Bakr"&gt;Abuu bakar&lt;/a&gt; maalinkii hijrada. Asmaa waxey ku dhimatay &lt;a href="Makaahiil"&gt;Makka&lt;/a&gt; &lt;a href="73H"&gt;73H&lt;/a&gt;, waxeyna ahayd &lt;a href="Muhaajiriin"&gt;Muhaajiriinta&lt;/a&gt; qofkii ugu dambeeyay ee dhimashada.
