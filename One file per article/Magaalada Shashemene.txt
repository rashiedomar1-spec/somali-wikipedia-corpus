Shashemene City (aka Shashemene Kenema ) waa koox xirfadleyaal kubbadda cagta Itoobiya ah, oo ku taal magaalada Shashemene, Aagga Misraq Shewa, Gobolka Oromia .  Waxay ka ciyaaraan horyaalka Itoobiya , heerka ugu sarreeya ee kubbadda cagta ee Itoobiya.
