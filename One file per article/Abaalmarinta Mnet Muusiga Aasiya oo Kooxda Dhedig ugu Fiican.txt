Abaalmarinta Mnet Muusiga Aasiya oo Kooxda Dhedig ugu Fiican (여자 그룹상) waa abaal marin ay bixiso CJ E&M Pictures ( Mnet ).

Sawirka guuleystayaasha

Guulaystayaal badan

Laga bilaabo 2021, shan kooxood oo dumar ah ayaa helay laba ama in ka badan abaal-marin.

Sido kale firi

Wonder Girls (2008)

Brown Eyed Girls (2009)

2NE1 (2010)

Girls' Generation (2011, 2013, 2015)

Sistar (2012, 2014)

Twice (2016, 2018–19, 2021)

Red Velvet (2017)

Blackpink (2020)

Liiska abaal-marinnada warbaahinta ee lagu sharfayo haweenka
