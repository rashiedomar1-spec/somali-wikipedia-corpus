text:
Giraangiraha Daal

Taayirrada taayirada ee loo isticmaalo gawaarida iyo gawaarida iyo diyaaradaha, oo ka samaysan caag iyo noocyo badan, taayir loo isticmaalo sicir bararka hawada laguna beddelo taayirrada kale markii la isticmaalo,
saaxiibo badan ayaa baxa oo iibiya taayirada iyo cinjirka adduunka oo dhan.
