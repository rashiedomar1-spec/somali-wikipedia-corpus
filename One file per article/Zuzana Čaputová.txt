Zuzana Čaputová ( ku dhawaaqida Slovak: [ˈzuzana ˈtʂaputɔʋaː] ; née Strapáková ; dhashay 21 Juun 1973) waa siyaasi Slovak ah, qareen iyo u dhaqdhaqaaqa deegaanka. Waa madaxweynihii shanaad ee Slovakia , jagadaas oo ay haysay tan iyo 15 June 2019. Čaputová waa haweeneydii ugu horreysay ee qabta xilka madaxtinimada, sidoo kale waa madaxweynihii ugu da'da yaraa ee taariikhda Slovakia, oo la doortay da'da 45.
