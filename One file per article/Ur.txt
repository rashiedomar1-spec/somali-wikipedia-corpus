Ur
Ur (&lt;a href="ingiriis"&gt;ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;: Smell ama Olfaction) waa habka dareenka urinta &lt;a href="neef"&gt;neef&lt;/a&gt; ama &lt;a href="hawo"&gt;hawo&lt;/a&gt; soo gaadhay &lt;a href="san"&gt;san&lt;/a&gt;ka dadka ama &lt;a href="xayawaan"&gt;xayawaan&lt;/a&gt;ka. Kaasi ayaa marka ur soo gaarto u gudbiya &lt;a href="unug"&gt;unug&lt;/a&gt;yada urta ee &lt;a href="jidh"&gt;jidh&lt;/a&gt;ka.
