Dumarka raaxada ama gabdhaha raaxada waxay ahaayeen dumar iyo gabdho ay ku qasbeen addoonsiga galmada ciidamada Jaban ee Imbaraadooriga ah dalalkii iyo dhulalkii la qabsaday kahor iyo intii lagu jiray Dagaalkii Labaad ee Adduunka . [ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] Magaca "dumarka raaxada" waa tarjumaanka Jabbaan ianfu (慰安婦), [ 5 ] eray loo soo qaatay " dhillooyinka ". [ 6 ]
