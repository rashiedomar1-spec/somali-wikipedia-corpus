TV Globo (Organizações Globo) waa shabakad telefishan &lt;a href="Barasiil"&gt;Barasiil&lt;/a&gt; ah oo ay iska leedahay &lt;a href="Grupo%20Globo"&gt;Grupo Globo&lt;/a&gt;. Waxaa la aasaasay Abriil 26, 1965 &lt;a href="Roberto%20Marinho"&gt;Roberto Marinho&lt;/a&gt;.
TV Globo waa shabakada telefishinka ee ugu weyn &lt;a href="Ameerikada%20koonfureed"&gt;Ameerikada koonfureed&lt;/a&gt; iyo sidoo kale shabakada labaad ee telefishanka ugu weyn adduunka ee ka dambeeya &lt;a href="American%20Broadcasting%20Company"&gt;ABC&lt;/a&gt; iyo soo saaraha ugu weyn ee telenovelas.
