title:
Sanduuqa codadka

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=37945

text:
isku xidha Trachea iyo Phrynx. Xadhkaha codka waxaa ka mid ah qaab-dhismeedka sida Adams apple, glottis, cochlea ama larynx ama epiglottis iyo xadhkaha codka. Xadhkaha codku waxay toosiyaan hawada ka soo kacaysa sambabada ilaa xadhkaha codka, iyagoo soo saaraya dhawaaqyo hadal.
