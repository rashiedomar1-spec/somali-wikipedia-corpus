Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O'Connell ( / ɪ ʃ / EYE -lish ; [ 1 ] Diseembar 18, 2001) waa heesaa Maraykan ah. Waxay markii ugu horreysay heshay dareenka dadweynaha 2015-kii heesteedii ugu horreysay " Ocean Eyes ", oo markii dambe ay sii daysay Darkroom, oo ah sawirka Interscope Records . Heesta waxaa qoray oo soo saaray walaalkeed, Finneas O'Connell , oo ay had iyo jeer kala shaqeyso muusigga. Extended play Eilish ugu horeysay, Don't Smile at Me (2017), ganacsi ahaan wuu guulaystay.
