&lt;a href="2022"&gt;2022&lt;/a&gt; Cayaaraha Asia, oo sidoo kale loo yaqaan XIX Asiad (Shiine: Dhi Shiji Jiè Yàzhōu Yùndònghuì), ayaa noqon doona dhacdo isboortis ah oo lagu dabaaldego Hangzhou, Zhejiang, Shiinaha laga soo bilaabo Sibtambar 10 ilaa 25, 2022 . Hangzhou wuxuu noqon doonaa magaalada saddexaad ee Shiinaha oo marti galinaysa Cayaaraha Asia, ka dib Beijing &lt;a href="1990"&gt;1990&lt;/a&gt; iyo Guangzhou &lt;a href="2010"&gt;2010&lt;/a&gt;.
