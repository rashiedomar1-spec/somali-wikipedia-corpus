Alamsay Waa garoonka Kubada cagta labaad ee uguwayn &lt;a href="somaliland"&gt;somaliland&lt;/a&gt;. waxaa uu kuyaalaa magaalo madaxda gobolka &lt;a href="Togdheer"&gt;Togdheer&lt;/a&gt; ee &lt;a href="Burco"&gt;Burco&lt;/a&gt;. waxa garoonkani uu qaadaa daawadayaal gaadhaya ilaa 15000 kun oo daawade. waxa uu kuyaalaa badhtamaha magaalada Burco gaar ahaan waxa uu katir sanyahay xaafada &lt;a href="Baarsiigo"&gt;Baarsiigo&lt;/a&gt;.garoonka alamsay waxa lagu qabtay ciyaarihii Gobolda Somaliland ee ugu danbeeyay.
