Han So-hee (한소희; dhashay Lee So-hee (이소희); Nofeembar 18, 1994) waa atariishada Koonfur Kuuriya iyo moodel. Waxay bilawday shaqadeeda sidii jilaa taageere ee taxanaha telefishanka "&lt;a href="Money%20Flower"&gt;Money Flower&lt;/a&gt;" (2017), "&lt;a href="100%20Days%20My%20Prince"&gt;100 Days My Prince&lt;/a&gt;" (2018), iyo "&lt;a href="Abyss"&gt;Abyss&lt;/a&gt;" (2019) ka hor inta aan loo gudbin doorarka hogaanka ee taxanaha &lt;a href="JTBC"&gt;JTBC&lt;/a&gt; "&lt;a href="The%20World%20of%20the%20Married"&gt;The World of the Married&lt;/a&gt;" (2020) iyo "&lt;a href="Nevertheless"&gt;Nevertheless&lt;/a&gt;" (2021), iyo &lt;a href="Netflix"&gt;Netflix&lt;/a&gt;-soo saaray "&lt;a href="My%20Name"&gt;My Name&lt;/a&gt;" (2021). Doorkeeda &lt;a href="Liiska%20jilayaasha%20The%20World%20of%20the%20Married"&gt;Yeo Da-kyung&lt;/a&gt; ee T"he World of the Married" (2020) waxay heshay aqoonsi ballaaran.
