Maria Anna “Mia” Beyerl (waxay ku dhalatay Diseembar 26, &lt;a href="1900"&gt;1900&lt;/a&gt; magaalada Linz, waxay geeriyootay May 24, &lt;a href="1989"&gt;1989&lt;/a&gt; Linz) – heesaaga opera-ga ee &lt;a href="Awstriya"&gt;Awstriya&lt;/a&gt;, biyaanoste iyo macalin muusig. Waxay xubin ka ahayd kooxda Volksoper Vienna. Sannadihii &lt;a href="1950"&gt;1950&lt;/a&gt;-aadkii, Mia Beyerl waxay noqotay mid ka mid ah &lt;a href="https%3A//www.jw.org/so/"&gt;Markhaatiyaasha Yehowah&lt;/a&gt;.
