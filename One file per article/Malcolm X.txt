Malcolm X , markii dambe la baxay "Malik Shabazz" ( / l k əm ˈ ɛ k s / ( carabi : الحاجّ مالك الشباز‎) (dhashay Maayo 19, 1925 – Febraayo 21, 1965) wuxuu ahaa hogaamiye diineed iyo dhaqdhaqaaqe bulsho u dhashay Dadka Madoow ee wadanka Maraykanka .


# Infobox

- **Ku dhashay**: Malcolm Yare Maayo 5, 1925 Omaha, Nebraska , U.S.
- **Dhimasho**: Febraayo 21, 1965 New York City , New York , U.S.
- **Sababta dhimashada**: La diley (xabad lala dhacay)
- **Ku aasan**: Xabaalaha Ferncliff
- **Naanees**: el-Hajj Malik el-Shabazz (الحاجّ مالك الشباز)
- **Shaqada**: Daaci, hogaamiye
- **Ururka**: Bulshada Islam , Muslim Mosque, Inc., Ururka Midowga Afrikan-Amerikanka
- **Dhererka**: 6 ft 3 in
- **Miisaanka**: 180 lb
- **Dhaqdhaqaaqa siyaasadeed**: Dhaqdhaqaaqa dadka Madow, Pan-Africanism
- **Diinta**: Sunni Islam (ka soo bedeshay Bulshada Islam )
- **Xaasaska**: Betty Shabazz (m. 1958)
- **Ilmaha**: Attallah Shabazz Qubilah Shabazz Ilyasah Shabazz Gamilah Lumumba Shabazz Malikah Shabazz Malaak Shabazz
- **Waalidka**: Earl Little, Louise Helen Norton Little
