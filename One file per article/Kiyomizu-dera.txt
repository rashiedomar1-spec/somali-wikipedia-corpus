Kiyomizu-dera

text:
Kiyomizu-dera(清水寺) waa macbad ku yaal Kiyomizu, Higashiyama Ward, Magaalada &lt;a href="Kyoto"&gt;Kyoto&lt;/a&gt;, macbadka madaxa ee dariiqada Kita Hosso ee Buddhism . Magaca buurtu waa Mt. Otowa. Shayga ugu muhiimsan ee cibaadadu waa Kanzeon Bodhisattva kan kow iyo toban weji leh, oo hubaysan . Si rasmi ah, waxaa loo yaqaan Otowasan Kiyomizudera . Markii hore waxay ka tirsanaan jirtay dariiqada Hosso , laakiin hadda way madax bannaan tahay oo isku magacawda Kita Hosso. Saigoku Sanjusansho No. 16 Fudasho. Macbadyada 10aad ilaa 14aad ee Luoyang Soddon iyo Saddex Macbadyo Kannon Xajka. Agagaarka goobta (aagga goobta) waa qiyaastii 130,000 mitir oo laba jibbaaran .
