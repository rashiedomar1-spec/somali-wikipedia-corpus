Af Kinyarwanda waa luqad laga hadlo Ruwanda , Yugandha iyo Jamhuuriyada Dimuqaraadiga Kongo . Af Kirundi way isku dhow yihiin.

Laga soo qaaday Baaqa Caalamiga ee Xuquuqda Aadanaha

Abantu bose bavuka aliko bakwiye agaciro no kwubahwa kimwe. Bose bavukana ubwenge n'umutima, bagomba kugilirana kivandimwe. [ 2 ]

Tarjumad
