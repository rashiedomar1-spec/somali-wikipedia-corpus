title:
Xadka waridda dharka

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=40197

text:
Xadka waridda dharka ( Af Ingiriis : clothing line ama washing line ; Af Carabi : لاسعانود ‎) waa qalab loo isticmaalo warka dharka sii dharkaas u noqdaan wax qalalan iyo engego. Xadka waridda dharka marmar waxaa lagu wara guriga dhexdeed, marmar na waxaa lagu wara banaanka. Xadka waridaa wuu kaga nadhiif badan yahay mashiinka qalashada. Xadka waridda wuu na kaga samaroon yahay mashiinka qalashada dhanka jawiga dabiiciga. [ 1 ]
