Maxamed cismaan waa mid kamida beelaha cismaan yoonis ee muse abokor habar jeclo waxayna walalo yihiin bare cismaan(reer bare) Maxamed Cismaan waa mid kamida beelaha Reeryoonis ee muse abokor habar jeclo waxayna dhalasho wadaag yihiim Maxamed cismaan waa reer kamida reer ugu taarikhda, tirada ido degaanda wayn beela habarjeclo

Dadka kasoo jeeda

. Waxa ka dhashay Sheekha ugu tariikhada wayn gayga somalidu tegto Soona gaadhsiyay Diinta gobolada Waqooyi  SH. ALI Warsame Xasan. waan Sheekhi ugu horeeyay ee Jamacad Al Madina taxa kuso bartay 1757 kii. Abaandule Kitte fiqi, IYO  BULXAN I.W.M Cismaan yoonis waxay

dhulka ay degaan

dagaan TOGHEER SOOL, SANAAG, SARAAR IYO HARAGYSA. Maxamed Cisamaan yoonis waa reer kaliye Habarjeclo ama Habar xabuusha Golaa deeganka ee Casimad Hargaysa kalsoo baxaa Xildhibaano.
