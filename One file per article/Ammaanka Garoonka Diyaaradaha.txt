ammaanka garoonka diyaaradaha

Amniga garoonka diyaaradaha ee laga bilaabo baarkinka iyo hubinta baasaboorka gudaha, amniga garoonka shaqada 24 saac iyo ka shaqeynta hubinta bacaha iyo dadka ka hor duulista.
