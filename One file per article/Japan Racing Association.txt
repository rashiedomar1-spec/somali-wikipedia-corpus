Isbahaysiga Fardaha ee &lt;a href="Jabaan"&gt;luqada Japanese&lt;/a&gt; (日本中央競馬会) oo ah hay'ad ka hadasha tartanka khayaaliga ah ee waafaqsan Sharciga 158 ee &lt;a href="1948"&gt;1948&lt;/a&gt;. Waa shirkad gaar loo leeyahay oo ay dawlada jabaan ay maalgashato. The follower waa Waaxda Xanaanada Xoolaha iyo Horse Racing, Place of Production, Wasaaradda Beeraha iyo Dhirta Haddii si kale loo dhigo, Japan Racing Association waa qabanqaabiye ka hadlaya kaymaha .
