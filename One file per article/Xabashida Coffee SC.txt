Ethiopian Coffee Sport Club (&lt;a href="Amxaari"&gt;Amxaari&lt;/a&gt; : የኢዮጵያ ቡና ስፖርት ክለብ), oo loo yaqaano Ethiopian Buna, waa naadi &lt;a href="Kubadda%20Cagta"&gt;kubbadda cagta&lt;/a&gt; ee Ethiopian Coffee, xarunteeduna tahay &lt;a href="Addis%20Abeba"&gt;Addis Ababa&lt;/a&gt; .
Waxay xubin ka yihiin xiriirka kubbadda cagta Itoobiya waxayna ka ciyaaraan heerka ugu sarreeya ee kubbadda cagta Itoobiya, Premier League ee Itoobiya . Garoonkoodu waa Addis Ababa Stadium . Iyada oo ay weheliso xafiiltanka &lt;a href="Saint%20George%20SC"&gt;Saint George&lt;/a&gt;, waxay ku faantaa mid ka mid ah saldhigyada taageereyaasha ee ugu weyn Itoobiya.
