Raadhiyaam

Raadhiyaam (&lt;a href="ingiriis"&gt;ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;: Radium) astaanta (&lt;a href="Ra"&gt;Ra&lt;/a&gt;) waa &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiye&lt;/a&gt; kimiko oo tirada &lt;a href="atom"&gt;atom&lt;/a&gt;kiisu tahay sideetan-iyo-sideed (88).
Curiyaha Raadhiyaam waa &lt;a href="bir"&gt;bir&lt;/a&gt; ka tirsan &lt;a href="Birta%20Alkalineta%20Dhulka"&gt;Birta Alkalineta Dhulka&lt;/a&gt; ee &lt;a href="guruubka%202"&gt;guruubka 2&lt;/a&gt;aad ee &lt;a href="Diwaanka%20Curiye"&gt;Diwaanka Curiye&lt;/a&gt;. 
Raadhiyaamta saafiga ah waxay leedahay &lt;a href="midab"&gt;midab&lt;/a&gt; cad, laakiin waxay inta badan la &lt;a href="falgal"&gt;falgal&lt;/a&gt;shaa &lt;a href="Ogsajiin"&gt;Ogsajiin&lt;/a&gt; taas oo u yeesha midab madoow ah. 
Dhamaan isotopeyada Raadhiyaam waa radioactive leh cimri dhan &lt;a href="sanad"&gt;1610 sano&lt;/a&gt;. Isotopeka kali ah ee leh elektaroono dagan waa raadhiyaam-226. 
Curiyaha Raadhiyaam waxaa helay labo &lt;a href="saynis"&gt;saynis&lt;/a&gt;yahan Marie Curie iyo Pierre Curie &lt;a href="sanad"&gt;sanadkii 1898&lt;/a&gt;.
