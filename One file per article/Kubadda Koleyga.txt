Kubadda Koleyga ("basketball")

HOLA ME LLAMO ADRIAN
Kubadda Kolayga[2] (laga bilaabo kubbadda koleyga Ingiriiska; laga bilaabo dambiisha, 'dambiil', iyo kubbad, 'kubad'), sidoo kale loo yaqaan kubbadda koleyga, kubbadda koleyga, ama kubbadda koleyga si fudud, [n. 1] waa ciyaar kooxeed oo ay ciyaaraan laba kooxood oo ka kooban shan ciyaartoy midkiiba afar toban daqiiqo oo rubuci ah, [4] laba iyo toban daqiiqo rubuci kasta gudaha NBA. Ujeeddada kooxdu waa in ay dhibco ku dhaliyaan kubbadda koofiyadda, giraanta 3.05 mitir (10 cagood) ka sarraysa barxadda kaas oo shabaggu ka soo laadlaadsan yahay. Dhibcaha dambiil walba waa labo ama seddex dhibcood, iyadoo ku xiran booska laga soo tuuray, ama hal dhibic haddii ay tahay tuurasho xor ah sababtoo ah qalad uu sameeyay ciyaaryahan ka soo horjeeda. Kooxda badisa waa kooxda ugu dhibcaha badan.
Kubadu waa in gacanta lagu xidhaa waa inay ahaato mid joogto ah oo xidhiidhsan. Ciyaartoyda, oo sidoo kale loo yaqaan ciyaartoyda kubbadda koleyga, kama dhaqaaqi karaan hal dhinac ilaa dhinaca kale ee kubadda haysta, laakiin waxay ku durayaan dhulka. Kooxda kubbada haysata, ama kooxda weerarka qaadaysa, waxay isku daydaa inay dhibco ku dhaliso toogashada, baabuur wadista, ama dunta, halka kooxda difaacana ay isku daydo inay taas ka hortagto iyagoo kubbadda xadaya ama samaynta baloogyada. Marka darbadii dambiishu ay fashilanto, ciyaartoyda labada kooxood waxay isku dayaan inay qabtaan soo laabashada.
