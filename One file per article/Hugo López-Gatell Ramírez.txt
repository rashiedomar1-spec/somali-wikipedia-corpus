Hugo López-Gatell Ramírez (Mexico City, Feebarwari 22, 1969) waa aqoonyahan ku takhasusay cilmiga faafa ee Mexico, cilmi baadhe, borofisar iyo sarkaal dowladeed. Ilaa iyo bishii Disembar 1, 2018, wuxuu madax u ahaa Undersecretariat ee Ka Hortagga iyo Dhiirrigelinta Caafimaadka, ee Wasaaradda Caafimaadka Mexico
