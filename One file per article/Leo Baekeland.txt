Leo Baekeland (Noofambar 14, 1863 - Febraayo 23, 1944) wuxuu ahaa kimistar &lt;a href="Beljim"&gt;Beljim&lt;/a&gt; &lt;a href="maraykan"&gt;maraykan&lt;/a&gt; ah . Wuxuu caan ku ahaa abuurista waraaqda sawirka ee &lt;a href="Velox"&gt;Velox&lt;/a&gt; sanadkii &lt;a href="1893"&gt;1893&lt;/a&gt; iyo &lt;a href="Bakelite"&gt;Bakelite&lt;/a&gt; sanadkii &lt;a href="1907"&gt;1907&lt;/a&gt;. Waxaa loogu yeeray "Aabaha warshadaha &lt;a href="balaastik"&gt;balaastik&lt;/a&gt;ada " soo saaristiisa Bakelite, oo ah raqiis aanan dab ka kici karin iyo caagag , waxay calaamadeeysay bilowga warshadeeynta balaastikada casriga ah.
