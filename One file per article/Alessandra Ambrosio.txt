Alessandra Corine Ambrósio [ 1 ] ( Af-Bortaqiiska Barasiil: [aleˈsɐ̃dɾɐ ɐ̃ˈbɾɔzju] ; dhashay 11 Abriil 1981)  waa moodel reer Barasiil-Mareykan ah, atariisho, naqshadeeye moodada, ganacsade iyo shaqsiyadda telefishanka. Waxay caan ku tahay shaqadeeda Fiktooriya Sekret waxaana loo doortay af-hayeenka koowaad ee khadka PINK ee shirkadda. Ambrosio wuxuu ahaa Malaa'igtii Fiktooriya Sekret laga soo bilaabo 2004 illaa 2017.
