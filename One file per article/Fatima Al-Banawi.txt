Fatima Al-Banawi (فاطمة البنوي; [ˈfaːtˤma alˈbanawi] ) waa filim-qaade iyo atariisho u dhalatay Sucuudiga. [ 1 ] Waxaa aad loogu yaqaanaa agaasimaha iyo jilaayada taxanayaasha iyo filimada caanka ah ee talefishinada Barakah Meets Barakah , A Blink of an Eye iyo Paranormal .
