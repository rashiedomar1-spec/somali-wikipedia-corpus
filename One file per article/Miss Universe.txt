Miss Universe

Miss Universe waa gogoldhig qurux badan oo caalami ah oo ay maamusho hay'adda Miss Universe ee fadhigeedu yahay Maraykanka. [1] Waxaa lagu qabtaa in ka badan 190 dal oo adduunka ah oo ay arkeen in ka badan nus bilyan qof sannadkiiba. [2] Iyadoo la socota Miss World, Miss International, iyo Miss Earth, Miss Universe waa mid ka mid ah Afarta Weyn ee Caalamiga ah ee quruxda badan.
Hay'adda Miss Universe Organization iyo calaamadeynta ayaa hadda leh, oo ay ku jiraan Miss USA iyo Miss Teen USA, oo ay bixiso wakaaladda WME / IMG.
Miss Universe oo hadda ah Demi-Leigh Nel-Peters oo ka socda Koonfur Afrika, ayaa lagu abaalmariyay November 26, 2017 magaalada Las Vegas, Nevada, Maraykanka.
