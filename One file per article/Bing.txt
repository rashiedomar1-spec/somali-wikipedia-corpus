Kani waa maqaal ku saabsan barta waxraadinta internetka ee Shirkada Microsoft .

Bing (wakhtiyadii hore loo yaqaanay Live Search , Windows Live Search , iyo MSN Search ) waa Bar Wax Raadin (midaasi oo lagu xayeeysiiyo ineey tahay "bar raadin go'aan qaadan karta" [ 3 ] ) leedahay shirkada caanka ah ee Microsoft .

Bing waxaa si shaaric ah ugu dhawaaqay madaxa sare ee Microsoft Steve Ballmer bishii Maay0 28, 2009. [ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 6 ]
