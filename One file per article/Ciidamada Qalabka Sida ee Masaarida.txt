title:
Ciidamada Qalabka Sida ee Masaarida

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=15519

text:
Ciidamada Qalabka Sida ee Masaarida yihiin ciidamada militariga Masar. Ciidamada Qalabka Sida ee Masaarida waa ugu weyn Africa, iyo Bariga Dhexe. Waxaa la aasaasay 1922, oo ka kooban Masaari ah Ciidanka Masri Navy, Masaarida Air Force oo Masaari ah difaaca cirka Ciidamada.
