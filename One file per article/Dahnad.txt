Geometry ahaan, Dahnad ( Af Ingiriis : vertex ) , waa goobta ay labo khad wada kulmaan. Muddada afar-geesood wuxuu leyahay afar dahnad. Muddadda shan-geesood wuxuu leyahay san dahnad. Muddadda lix geesood waxay leedahay lix dahnad. [ 1 ] [ 2 ]
