Warshadda Dharka ee Balacad waa warshad ku taalo degamada &lt;a href="Balcad"&gt;Balcad&lt;/a&gt; ee gobolka &lt;a href="Shabeellaha%20Dhexe"&gt;Shabeelada Dhexe&lt;/a&gt;.
