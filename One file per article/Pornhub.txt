Pornhub , qayb ka mid ah ololaha Pornhub NETWORK, waa website video wadaagga websedyada ah iyo goobta ugu weyn lulataaye internet-ka. [ 2 ] [ 3 ] Bilaabey 2007 iyo salaysan in Montreal, Quebec, Pornhub waa, website a ad taageerto lacag la'aan ah, kaas oo u ogolaanaya dadka isticmaala ay ku daawan Filimada lagu xirfad leh, marka lagu daro Filimada lagu hiwaayadda. Pornhub sidoo kale xafiisyo ku leedahay iyo kiis ee San Francisco, California, Houston, Texas, New Orleans, ka mudan iyo London, Midowga boqortooyada Britan . Bishii Maarso 2010 , Pornhub soo iibsaday by MindGeek (oo horay loogu Manwin), oo leh tiro bogagga internetka ee kale lulataaye. [ 4 ]
