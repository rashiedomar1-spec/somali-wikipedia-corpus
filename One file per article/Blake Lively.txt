Blake Ellender Lively (dhashay Agoosto 25, 1987 [ 1 ] )  waa atariisho Mareykan ah, moodeel, agaasime, iyo u doode. Waxay ku dhalatay Los Angeles , Lively waa gabadha uu dhalay jilaaga Ernie Lively , waxayna ku soo bandhigtay doorkeeda ugu horeysay ee Sandman (1998), ka dib markii ay jishay shaqooyin badan, Lively waxay heshay aqoonsi ah muujinta dorkeeda sida Serena van der Woodsen ee taxanaha telefishanka CW Gossip Girl (2007–2012).

Lively waxay guursatay jilaaga Ryan Reynolds , kaasoo ay u dhashay saddex gabdhood.
