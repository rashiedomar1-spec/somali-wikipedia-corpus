Da' (; ) waa nidaamka &lt;a href="jidh"&gt;jidh&lt;/a&gt;ka &lt;a href="noole"&gt;noolaha&lt;/a&gt; u koro intii wakhti ah isla markaana u &lt;a href="gaboow"&gt;gaboow&lt;/a&gt;o. Nooluhu marka uu weynaado ee intii mudo ah joogo wuxuu noqdaa "&lt;a href="Duq"&gt;Duq&lt;/a&gt;" taasi oo &lt;a href="saynis"&gt;saynis&lt;/a&gt; ahaan u taagan isbedelka ku imanaya jidhka. 
Marka laga hadlayo dadka, da'da waa isbedelka ku yimaada jidhka qofka intii wakhti ah, taasi oo ugu dambeynta keenaysa isbedel ku yimaada muuqaalka jidhka, fikirka, shaqada, awooda iyo dhaqanka bulsho ee qofkaasi.
