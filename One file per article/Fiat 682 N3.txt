Fiat 682 N3 Waa gaadiid xamuulka qaada oo ay soo saari jirtay Shirkadda Fiyaat intii u dhaxaysay 1950 ilaa 1988.

N3 Matoorkiisu waa 6 Bustin Fiat 203 ee 11.548 Sentimitir cubo cm 3 iyo 140 CV oo loo rogay 1.900 g/min.

Waana gaarigii ugu horreeyey ee ay soo saarto Fiyaat oo rimoor jiida.
