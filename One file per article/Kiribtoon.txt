Kiribtoon (; ) astaanta (&lt;a href="Kr"&gt;Kr&lt;/a&gt;) waa &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiye&lt;/a&gt; kimiko oo leh tiro &lt;a href="atom"&gt;atom&lt;/a&gt; sodan-iyo-lix ah. Waxay xubin ka tahay &lt;a href="guruubka%2018"&gt;guruubka 18&lt;/a&gt;aad ee &lt;a href="Diwaanka%20Curiye"&gt;Diwaanka Curiye&lt;/a&gt; iyo &lt;a href="neef"&gt;neef&lt;/a&gt;ta loo yaqaano &lt;a href="Neefta%20Gobta"&gt;Neefta Gobta&lt;/a&gt; (Noble Gas).
