Sana Minatozaki (湊崎 紗夏; Minatozaki Sana ; dhashay Diseembar 29, 1996) [ 1 ] , loo yaqaano Sana ( Kuuriya : 사나 ; Af Jabaniis : サナ ), waa heesaa reer Jabaan ah oo fadhigeedu yahay Koonfur Kuuriya. Iyadu waa xubin ka mid ah kooxda gabdhaha K-pop Twice , oo ay samaysay JYP Entertainment . Sana waa mid ka mid ah saddex xubnood ee Twice oo Jabbaan ah. [ 2 ] [ 3 ]
