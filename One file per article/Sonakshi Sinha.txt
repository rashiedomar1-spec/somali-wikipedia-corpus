Sonakshi Sinha ( loogu dhawaaqo [soːnaːkʂi sɪnɦa] ; 2 Juun 1987) waa atariisho iyo heesaa Hindi ah oo ka shaqeysa filimada Hindiga. [ 2 ] Ka dib markii ay ka shaqeysay dharka nashqadeeya filimada madaxa banaan , waxay soo bandhigtay filimkeedii ugu horreeyay iyadoo ku muujisay Rajjo Pandey filimkii Dabangg sanadkii 2010. [ 3 ] Waxay u soo kacday inay caan ku noqoto ciyaarista xiisaha jacaylka jilayaasha ragga ee filimada Rowdy Rathore (2012), Son of Sardaar (2012), Holiday: A Soldier Is Never Off Duty (2014), iyo ka muuqashada item songs .
