title:
Ganacsi

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=5160

text:
Ganacsi waa soo iibso adna sii iibih intad macash saratoh.
Wuxuna u qeybsama 2nuuc oo kala ah;
1- inad badeecad iibiso iyo.
2- inad services bixisoh.
Wixi inta ka si hadhey waa dhac an lo meel dayin ee ka fogow dhaqalahagana ka ilaasho.
