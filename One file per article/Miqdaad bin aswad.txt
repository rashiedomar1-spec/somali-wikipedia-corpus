Miqdaad bin Camar oo loo yaqaan Miqdaad bin Aswad waa saxaabi ka mid ahaa dadkii ugu horeeyay ee islaamka qaata, wuxuu u &lt;a href="Hijrada%20xabasha"&gt;haajiray Xabasha&lt;/a&gt; ka dbina &lt;a href="Hijrada%20madiina"&gt;Madiina&lt;/a&gt;, Nabiga scw ayuuna kala qeyb galay intuu noolaa duulaamadiis dhamaan, waqtigii &lt;a href="Khulafada%20raashidiinta"&gt;Khulafada Raashidiinta&lt;/a&gt; wuxuu qeyb ka noqday &lt;a href="Furashada%20shaam"&gt;Furshada shaam&lt;/a&gt; iyo &lt;a href="Furshada%20masar"&gt;Masar&lt;/a&gt;. Miqdaad sandkii &lt;a href="33H"&gt;33H&lt;/a&gt; ayuu ku dhintay &lt;a href="Madiina%20Al-munawara"&gt;Madiina&lt;/a&gt;.
