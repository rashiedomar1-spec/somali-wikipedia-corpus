Emily Jean " Emma " Stone (dhashay Nofeembar 6, 1988) waa atariisho Mareykan ah. Waa qofka helay abaal-marinno kala duwan, oo ay ku jiraan Abaalmarinta Akadeemiyada , Abaalmarinta Filimada Akadeemiyada Ingiriiska , Abaalmarinta Jilayaasha Shaashada , iyo Abaalmarinta Golden Globe .  Sannadkii 2017, Stone waa aktarada adduunka ugu mushaarka badan.
