&lt;a href="Svalbard"&gt;Svalbard&lt;/a&gt; (/ ˈsvɑːlbɑːr/ SVAHL-bar, Urban East Norwegian: [ˈsvɑ̂ːɫbɑr] (waxa ku saabsan dhawaqahan)), oo sidoo kale loo yaqaan Spitsbergen, ama Spitzbergen, waa jasiirado &lt;a href="Norway"&gt;Norway&lt;/a&gt; ah oo ku yaal Badweynta Arctic. Waqooyiga dhul weynaha &lt;a href="Yurub"&gt;Yurub&lt;/a&gt;, waxay qiyaastii badhtamaha u dhaxaysa xeebta waqooyi ee Norway iyo cirifka woqooyi.
