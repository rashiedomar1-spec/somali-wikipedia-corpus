My Wife Got Married waa 2008 filim jaceyl oo Koonfur Kuuriya. Waxaa agaasime ka ah Jeong Yoon-soo oo ay jilayaan Kim Joo-hyuk iyo Son Ye-jin doorka hoggaamineed, filimkan waxaa laga soo min-guuriyay sheeko isku mid ah oo uu qoray Park Hyun-wook. [ 1 ]
