Ray-Ban
Ray-Ban waa calaamada muraayadaha indhaha iyo muraayadaha muraayadaha oo la aasaasay 1937 shirkadda American Bausch &amp; Lomb. Nooca ugu fiican ayaa loo yaqaano jidadkooda Wayfarer iyo Aviator ee muraayadaha indhaha. Sanadkii 1999-kii, Bausch &amp; Lomb ayaa markasta u iibiyey kooxda &lt;a href="Talyaaniga"&gt;Talyaaniga&lt;/a&gt; ee Luxottica Group oo ah $ 640 milyan oo doolar.
