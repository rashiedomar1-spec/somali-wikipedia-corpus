Cher Lloyd (dhashay 28 Luuliyo 1993) [ 1 ] [ 2 ] waa heesaa Ingiriis ah. [ 3 ] Lloyd waxay u saxeexday Syco Music . Heesteedii ugu horeysay, "Swagger Jagger ", ayaa la sii daayay Luuliyo 2011.

Fanka

Lloyd waxay shaaca ka qaaday wareysiga inay taageere u tahay Dolly Parton . [ 4 ] Lloyd waxay soo xigatay fanaanada caanka ah ee Nicki Minaj inay saamayn ku leedahay. [ 5 ]
