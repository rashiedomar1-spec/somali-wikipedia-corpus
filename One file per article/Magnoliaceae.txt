Magnoliaceae waa qoys ka mid ah magnolia . Magnoliaceae waxaa iska leh nidaamka dhirta magnolia, amarka Magnoliopsida, madaxa Tracheophyta, iyo boqortooyada dhirta.  Qoyska Magnoliaceae waxay daboolayaan 314 generas, marka loo eego Buugga Nolosha. [ 1 ]

Kaladogaraam sida ku cad Buugga Nolosha waa:

Liriodendron

Magnolia

Annonaceae

Degeneriaceae

Eupomatiaceae

Himantandraceae

Myristicaceae

Tixraac
