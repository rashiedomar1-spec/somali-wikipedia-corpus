Aakharo [ 2 ] (Magac Saynis: Indigofera schimperi Jaub. & Spach) ama dharjo ama dharqo waa dhir laga heli karo Soomaaliya , qaybo ka mid ah Bariga Afrika iyo Koonfurta Afrika , Yemen iyo Ustareliya . [ 3 ] Geed gaaban uu dhidhir ilaa 1.5 mitir leh uu yahay [ 4 ] .
