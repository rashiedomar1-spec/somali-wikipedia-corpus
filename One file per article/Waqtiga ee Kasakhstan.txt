Waqtiga caadiga ah ee &lt;a href="Kasakhstan"&gt;Kasakhstan&lt;/a&gt; maaha UTC+05:00 ama UTC+06:00. Waqtiyadaas waxaa loo yaqaan &lt;a href="Waqtiga%20Aqtobe"&gt;Waqtiga Aqtobe&lt;/a&gt; (AQTT) ama Waqtiga Alma-Ata (ALMT). Kasakhstan ma ilaaliso wakhtiga badbaadinta iftiinka maalinta.
