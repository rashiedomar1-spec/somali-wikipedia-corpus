STAYC ( Hangul : 스테이씨) waa koox gabdho Koonfur Kuuriya ah oo ay samaysay High Up Entertainment . Kooxdan ayaa ka kooban lix xubnood waxayna sii daayeen albumkoodii ugu horeeyay Star to a Young Culture ee 2020. [ 1 ]

Magaca

Magaca kooxda, STAYC, waa erey la soo gaabiyey ( S tar t o a Y oung C ulture "Xiddiga Dhaqanka Dhallinyarada") waxaana loola jeedaa "in la muujiyo ujeeddada falka ee lagu maamulayo dhaqanka pop". [ 2 ]
