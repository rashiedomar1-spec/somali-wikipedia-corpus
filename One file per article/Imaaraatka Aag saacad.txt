Imaaraatka Aag saacad

url:

text:
Imaaraatka Aag saacad +4

Imaaraatka Heerka Waqtiga ama Waqtiga UAE Waqtiga Aagga waa aagga waqtiga ee Imaaraadka Imaaraadka Carabta. Waxaa la bixiyaa Gulf Standard Time, isagoo 4 saacadood ka horeya GMT / UTC (UTC + 04: 00) waana isku xiran yahay wadada deriska ah ee Cumaan.
