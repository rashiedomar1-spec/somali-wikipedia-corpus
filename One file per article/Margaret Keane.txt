&lt;a href="Margaret%20Keane"&gt;Margaret Keane&lt;/a&gt;, dhashay Peggy Doris Hawkins (wuxuu ku dhashay Sebtembar 15, 1927 ee &lt;a href="Nashville"&gt;Nashville&lt;/a&gt;) - farshaxan-yaqaan Maraykan ah. Waxay caan ka heshay adduunka oo dhan iyadoo rinjiyeynaysa sawirrada dadka (gaar ahaan carruurta) oo leh sifo "indho waaweyn". Wuxuu inta badan sawiraa carruurta, haweenka iyo xoolaha. Waa &lt;a href="https%3A//www.jw.org/so/"&gt;Markhaati Yehowah&lt;/a&gt;.
