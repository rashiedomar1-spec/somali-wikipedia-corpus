"Kani waa maqaal ku saabsan guud ahaan Gaadiidka. Maqaalo kale fiiri &lt;a href="Baabuur"&gt;Baabuur&lt;/a&gt; (&lt;a href="Gaadhi"&gt;Gaadhi&lt;/a&gt;)"
Gaadiid (&lt;a href="Ingiriis"&gt;Ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;: vehicle; &lt;a href="carabi"&gt;carabi&lt;/a&gt;: مركبة) waa aalad mashiin ah oo qof ama &lt;a href="labo"&gt;labo&lt;/a&gt; jiheeynayaan oo loo isticmaalo in lagu safriyo dad ama alaabo. Guud ahaan kalmada Gaadiid waa magaca la isku yidhaahdo dhamaan &lt;a href="baabuur"&gt;baabuur&lt;/a&gt;ta, &lt;a href="diyaarad"&gt;diyaarad&lt;/a&gt;aha, &lt;a href="tareen"&gt;tareen&lt;/a&gt;ada, &lt;a href="Markab"&gt;markabyada&lt;/a&gt;, mootooyinka iyo &lt;a href="Bushkuleeti"&gt;bushkuleetiyaasha&lt;/a&gt;, laakiin si gaar ah waxaa loo yaqaanaa aaladaha dad-sameega ah ee mara laamiyada. Sida dhamaan noocyada &lt;a href="baabuur"&gt;baabuur&lt;/a&gt;ta, &lt;a href="tareen"&gt;tareen&lt;/a&gt;ada IWM.
Guud ahaan Gaadiidku wuxuu isticmaalaa &lt;a href="biyo"&gt;biyo&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="shidaal"&gt;shidaal&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="saliid"&gt;saliid&lt;/a&gt; iyo waxyaabo kale. Gaadiidku wuxuu maraa laamiyada, &lt;a href="biyo"&gt;biyo&lt;/a&gt;ha (sida &lt;a href="bad"&gt;bad&lt;/a&gt;aha, &lt;a href="webi"&gt;webi&lt;/a&gt;yada iyo &lt;a href="har"&gt;har&lt;/a&gt;aha waaweyn) dushooda iyo [[cir]ka dhexdiisa.
Guud ahaan gaadiidka waxaa laga isticmaalaa meel kasta oo dunida ka mid.
