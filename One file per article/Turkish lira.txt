Lire

Lire Turkish (Turkish: Turkish lirası; calaamad: ₺; Koodh: DAWLO, sida caadiga loo soo gaabiyo TL) [2] waa lacagta Turkiga iyo Jamhuuriyadda Turkiga ee is-kaashatey ee waqooyiga Cyprus. Lacagta Turkiga waxaa loo qaybiyaa 100 kuruş.
