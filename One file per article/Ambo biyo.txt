Ambo Mineral Water
Ambo Mineral Water waa caan ka mid ah biyaha macdanta leh ee dhalooyinka laga dhaliyo, oo laga heley ilaha Ambo Senkele, meel u dhow magaalada Ambo ee bartamaha Itoobiya. Waa cabitaan caan ah oo ka jira &lt;a href="Itoobiya"&gt;Itoobiya&lt;/a&gt;, waxaana lagu qeexay "biyaha macaan ee ugu da'da casriga casriga ah" iyo Itoobiya oo ah "dhalada ugu da'da yar macdanta biyaha." Waa la dhalaalay ilaa &lt;a href="1930"&gt;1930&lt;/a&gt; Shirkadda waxaa iska leh SABMiller PLC iyo SouthWest Development, waxaana loo isticmaalaa inay noqoto shirkad dowladeed.
