Somaliland

Ama: Madaxbannaanida

Madaxbanaanida Somaliland oo ah wadan ku yaala bariga afrika oo ku kulmaya shirkii montevideo oo ictiraaf ku dhaw ayaa wadamo badan ayaa Somaliland ku taageera dal madax banaan waxaana loo yaqaanaa wadan ku dhaw in loo aqoonsado qaran.
