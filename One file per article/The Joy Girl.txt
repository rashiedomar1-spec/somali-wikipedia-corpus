The Joy Girl

text:
The Joy Girl waa filim aamusnaan ah oo Mareykan ah oo 1927 oo uu agaasime ka ahaa Allan Dwan, oo ay soo saartay Fox Film Corporation, oo ay jilayeen Olive Borden , Neil Hamilton, iyo Marie Dressler, waxayna ku saleysan tahay sheekada gaaban ee isla magaca oo May Edginton. [ 1 ]

Tixraacyo
