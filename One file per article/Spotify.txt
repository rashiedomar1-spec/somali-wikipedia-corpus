Spotify (/ˈspɒtɪfaɪ/) waa adeeg baahinta muusiga khadka tooska ah. Waxa ay bixisa in ka badan 30 milyan hees.
=Helitaanka=
Spotify waxaa laga heli karaa 61 waddan, oo ay ku jiraan "Austria", "Belgium", "Denmark", "Faroe Islands", "Finland", "France", "Germany", "the Netherlands", "Norway", "Spain", "Sweden", "Switzerland", Boqortooyada Ingiriiska, Hindiya iyo Maraykanka.
