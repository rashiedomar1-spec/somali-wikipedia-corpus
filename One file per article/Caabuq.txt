Eubacteria [ 3 ]

Caabuq ( Af Ingiriis : infection ) waa duulaan ka yimid nafleyda yaryar ee cudurley ah iyo balaadhsigood marka ay jirka soo galaan.

Talaalooyin iyo dawooyin fara badan ayaa jira ee caabuq lagu dawayn karo, sida talaalka antibiotiks, talaalka antiviral, talaalka antifungal, talaalka antiprotozoal, iyo talaalka antihelminthik. Cudurada ee caabuqa ah waxay midha-dhaleen dhimasho tiradooda gaadhaysa 9.2 milyan qof ee lagu qiyaasay sanadka 2013. [ 4 ] [ 5 ]
