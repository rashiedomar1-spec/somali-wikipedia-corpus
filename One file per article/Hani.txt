Ahn Hee-yeon ( Hangul : 안희연; Maajo 1, 1992),  xirfad ahaan loo yaqaan Hani , waa heesaa iyo atariisho Koonfur Kuuriya ah. Waxaa loo yaqaanaa xubin ka tirsan kooxda gabdhaha EXID iyo koox-hoosaadkeeda, SoljiHani (oo hore loogu yiqiin Dasoni; Hangul : 다소니). Waxay ka soo muuqatay bandhigyada telefishinka Weekly Idol , Off to School , Crime Scene iyo A Style for You .
