2011 - 2012 - 2013

Sanadka 2012 waxa uu tixraacayaa wakhti gaar ah oo ku jira kalandarka Gregorian-ka, laga bilaabo Janaayo 1 ilaa Diseembar 31, 2012. Waa sanadka 2012-aad ee Common Era (CE) waxaana uu ka tirsan yahay qarniga 21aad. Sanadkan waxa uu ka dambeeyaa 2011 wuxuuna ka horreeyaa 2013.
