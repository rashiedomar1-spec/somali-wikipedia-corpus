Hay'ada Haweenka ee Qaramada Midoobay ( Af Ingiriis : UN Women ; Af Carabi : لاسعانود ‎) sida dowliga ah loo yaqaano Waaxda Sinaanta Jinsi iyi Awoodaynta Dumarka ee Qaramada Midoobay ( United Nations Entity for Gender Equality and the Empowerment of Women ) waa urur hoostega Qaramada Midoobay kaasi oo loogu talogalay inuu quwad iyo taageero siiyo dumarka caalamka.

Ururkan Haweenka Qaramada Midoobay wuxuu shaqada bilaabay Janaayo 2011. [ 2 ]
