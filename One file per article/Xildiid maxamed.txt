Hildid is a real name of &lt;a href="horogle"&gt;horogle&lt;/a&gt; clan of mohamed gorgarte ( madhibaan ) this clan is subclan of madhibaan clans.
These clan habited in mudug region and dollo ethio and nugaal, specially districts in nugaal region, mudug region, and dollo region in dds that districts included, horogle town, lagu raqsey, balikhayr, togyar in ethiopia and other districts in somalia.
