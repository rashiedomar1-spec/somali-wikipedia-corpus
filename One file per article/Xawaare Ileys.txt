Xawaaraha Ileyska
Xawaaraha Ileyska ama Xawaaraha Iftiinka (&lt;a href="ingiriis"&gt;ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;: Speed of Light) waa &lt;a href="halbeeg"&gt;halbeeg&lt;/a&gt;a lagu cabiro orodka awooda iftiinka ama &lt;a href="ileys"&gt;ileys&lt;/a&gt;ku ku socdo &lt;a href="wakhti"&gt;wakhti&lt;/a&gt; kooban gudaheed. 
Inta la ogyahay, xawaaraha ugu dheereeya ee &lt;a href="awood"&gt;awood&lt;/a&gt; ama shay kale ku socon karo waa &lt;a href="Xawaare%20Ileys"&gt;Xawaaraha Ileyska&lt;/a&gt; kaas oo la mid ah 299,792,458 &lt;a href="mitir"&gt;mitir&lt;/a&gt; &lt;a href="ilbiriqsi"&gt;ilbiriqsi&lt;/a&gt;giiba (m/s), taas oo qiyaas ahaan u dhiganta 1079 &lt;a href="milyan"&gt;milyan&lt;/a&gt;
&lt;a href="kilomitir"&gt;kilomitir&lt;/a&gt; saacadiiba ama (671,000,000 mph).
