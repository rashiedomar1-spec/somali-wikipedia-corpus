Palermo Football Club ama Palermo waa koox-banooni oo kuyaalo magaalada Palermo ee dalka Talyaaniga .

Naadiga ayaa ciyaaray afar jeer koobka UEFA Cup. Finalka koobkii Talyaaniga (1974, 1979 iyo 2011).
