title:
Baalcad geesi

url:
https://so.wikipedia.org/wiki?curid=33184

text:
Gesi darood waa besha baalcad absame kumade kablalax daarood.

„Geesinimadu waa tayada xoog, geesinimo, iyo geesinimo. Macnuhu maaha inaadan waligaa cabsan. Taa beddelkeeda, waxay la macno tahay in tallaabo la qaado in kasta oo ay cabsideenna jirto. Yehowah waa isha geesinimada runta ah.”
