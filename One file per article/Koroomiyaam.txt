Koroomiyaam
Koroomiyaam (&lt;a href="ingiriis"&gt;ingiriis&lt;/a&gt;: Chromium) astaanta (&lt;a href="Cr"&gt;Cr&lt;/a&gt;) waa &lt;a href="curiye"&gt;curiye&lt;/a&gt; kimiko oo tirada &lt;a href="atom"&gt;atom&lt;/a&gt;kiisu tahay labaatan-iyo-afar (24). 
Curiyaha Koroomiyaam waa &lt;a href="bir"&gt;bir&lt;/a&gt; ka tirsan &lt;a href="Transitional%20Metal"&gt;Transitional Metal&lt;/a&gt;ska ee &lt;a href="guruubka%206"&gt;guruubka 6&lt;/a&gt;aad ee &lt;a href="Diwaanka%20Curiye"&gt;Diwaanka Curiye&lt;/a&gt;. 
Curiyahan waa &lt;a href="adke"&gt;adke&lt;/a&gt; la kala jabin karo oo leh midab cagaar iftiimaya taas oo leh bar dhalaalin aad u sareeysa.
