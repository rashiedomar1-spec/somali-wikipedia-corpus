Niini Dance (dhashay Nadiifa ) [ 1 ] waa fannaanad iyo raaber Soomaaliyeed, ku caan baxay heesaheeda, " Balaq Balaq ", " Labo Koob Caano ", " Quraacda Kari " iyo " 5ta Somali ".

Nolosha shaqsiyeed

Niini Dance waxay ku nooshahay Muqdisho waxayna u dhashay hal wiil.
