Simit

Simit, Carabi: Seynid, turjubaan. (Giriig: κουλούρι) waa riwaayad wareeg ah, oo sida caadiga ah lagu soo koobay abuurka sisinta ama, inta badan sida caadiga ah, boobka, boogaha, caanaha, kiriimyada ama gabbaldayaha, laga helaa cuntooyinka quraan ee Ottomankii hore, iyo cabirka Bariga Dhexe ee Simit, qadarin, ladnaan, iyo dabeecadaha kale way kala duwan yihiin gobollada. Waxaa loo yaqaan 'Bagel' ee Turkiga.
Iiraan, simit waxaa loo yaqaana nacas ("giigsan"), inkastoo ay aad ugu egtahay noocyada kala duwan ee Istanbul. Simit in Ankara way ka yaryihiin oo ka jilicsan yihiin magaalooyinka kale. Simit in Istanbul waxaa lagu sameeyey buro.
